## Speech/Remarks Collection Tips

Speeches and official remarks:

1. **C-SPAN (Best for US Political)**
   - https://www.c-span.org/
   - Search by speaker name and date
   - Full, unedited coverage
   - Transcripts often available

2. **Official Government Sites**
   - White House: Presidential remarks
   - Congress.gov: Floor speeches
   - State websites: Governor remarks

3. **Search Strategy**
   - "[Speaker] [event] full speech"
   - "[Speaker] remarks [location] [date]"
   - "[Speaker] address [topic]"

4. **Event-Specific Sources**
   - State of the Union: Multiple networks carry live
   - UN speeches: UN Web TV
   - Davos/WEF: World Economic Forum channel
   - March for Life, etc.: Event organizer channels

5. **Transcript vs Video**
   - Official transcripts are most accurate
   - Video transcripts may miss crowd reactions, pauses
   - For mention markets, either usually works
