Donald Trump: In the season of joy, we spend time with our families. We renew the bonds of love and goodwill between our citizens, and most importantly we celebrate the miracle of Christmas. For Christians we remember the story of Jesus, Mary, and Joseph, that began more than 2,000 years ago.
