Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening spoke with Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch and thanked the officers and commanders of the Israel Police for their determined and successful operation in foiling the action that was intended to be carried out at Ben-Gurion International Airport.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, “The deployment of the operational and diplomatic elements proved itself. Today, the world better understands where the true problems are in the Middle East.”
