Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today in Jerusalem, met with visiting Panamanian President Ricardo Martinelli, who emphasized his country's and people's friendship for Israel.

The two men discussed upgrading bilateral relations in all fields, including economic ties, agricultural cooperation, an agreement against double-taxation and consideration of air links.

Prime Minister Netanyahu proposed institutionalizing bilateral cooperation via a framework for ministerial consultations and President Martinelli welcomed the idea.
