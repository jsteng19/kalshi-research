Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this morning, convened a team of ministers and advisers and told them of his hard impression of the reality in which thousands of infiltrators arrive in Israel each month. He instructed those present to formulate a plan in the framework of which fines on those who employ persons in Israel illegally would be significantly increased.

Prime Minister Netanyahu yesterday evening, told the State Attorney's Office conference in Eilat that the border fence would be completed within a year and that it would receive a budgetary supplement.
