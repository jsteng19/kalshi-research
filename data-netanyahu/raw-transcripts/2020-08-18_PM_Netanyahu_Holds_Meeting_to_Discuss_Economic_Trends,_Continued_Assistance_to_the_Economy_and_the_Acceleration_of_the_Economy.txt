Prime Minister Netanyahu, held a meeting to discuss economic trends, continued assistance to the economy and the acceleration of the economy. Finance Minister Yisrael Katz, the Governor of the Bank of Israel, the Director General of the Prime Minister's Office, the Finance Ministry Budget Director, the Finance Ministry Accountant General, the Finance Ministry Chief Economist, the head of the National Economic Council, the Director of the Israel Tax Authority and other senior officials attended the meeting.

The Governor of the Bank of Israel, the Finance Ministry officials and other professionals briefed Prime Minister Netanyahu in detail. The Prime Minister requested that additional plans on returning workers to the employment cycle and on accelerating the economy be submitted forthwith.
