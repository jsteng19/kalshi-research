US President Barack Obama and Russian Prime Minister Vladimir Putin today, telephoned Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu in order to congratulate him on taking office.

US President Obama congratulated Prime Minister Netanyahu on his election and wished him success in office. The US President reiterated the US's unshakeable commitment to Israel's security. The approximately 30-minute conversation between the two men was friendly and constructive.

Russian Prime Minister Putin also congratulated Prime Minister Netanyahu on his election and wished him success in office. The two men discussed the strengthening of bilateral relations, regional security and the need to advance peace and stability in the Middle East. They agreed to meet in the near future. The Russian Prime Minister told Prime Minister Netanyahu, "You would be a welcome guest in Moscow."
