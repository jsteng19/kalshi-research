Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with British Cabinet Minister Michael Gove. The two discussed mutual recognition of the 'Green Passport' as well as the possibility of increasing cooperation in the research, development and production of vaccines against the coronavirus and against future viruses. Minister Gove noted that Israel and Britain have had great success in their vaccination campaigns and added that the latter is interested in learning from the Israeli experience in how to return to routine. They also agreed on moving forward on upgrading the bilateral free trade agreement.
