Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu yesterday met with IDF Home Front Command volunteers during his

visit to Command headquarters

. The volunteers were special needs youth suffering from Down's Syndrome and attention deficit-hyperactivity problems who are participating in a project led by Capt. Ariel Almog, in cooperation with Prof. Reuven Feuerstein's

International Center for the Enhancement of Learning Potential

The young volunteers told Prime Minister Netanyahu that they have been volunteering at various bases for over a decade. He expressed his appreciation and said that he views them as an example for Israeli society in general and for the IDF in particular.
