Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today: "I send my condolences to the family of former Mossad Director Zvi Zamir. Zvi led the most daring and significant operations in the annals of the Mossad, including Operation Wrath of G-d. These successful operations contributed greatly to the security of Israel and to ensuring its future. May his memory be blessed."
