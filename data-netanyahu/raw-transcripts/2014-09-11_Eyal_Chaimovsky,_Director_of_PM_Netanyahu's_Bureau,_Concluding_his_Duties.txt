The Director of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's bureau for the past two-and-a-half years, Eyal Chaimovsky, who has served since the Prime Minister's previous term, agreed with him that he would conclude his duties after the upcoming holidays.

Prime Minister Netanyahu commended Chaimovsky's professionalism, thanked him for his dedication and his considerable contributions to the work of the bureau, and wished him every future success.

Chaimovsky said, "I had the great privilege of working with the Prime Minister in such a significant period for the State of Israel."
