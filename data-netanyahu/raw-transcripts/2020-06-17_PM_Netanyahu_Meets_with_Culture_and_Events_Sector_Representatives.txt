Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/event_culture_meeting170620

Prime Minister Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with representatives of the culture and events sector and said at the start of the meeting:

"I very much want to listen to you and I very much want to help you. I know that there is distress; we want to deal with it. Therefore, I have also asked the Finance Minister to join us. We will listen and we will help to the best of our ability."Also attending the meeting were the Finance Minister, Prime Minister's Office Acting Director General, Finance Ministry Director General, the head of the National Economic Council, the Finance Ministry Budget Director, and senior performing arts, cultural and ancillary profession representatives
