Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding an economic consultation on the budget. Professional officials, the Accountant General and the head of the Finance Ministry Budget Division have claimed that a budget needs to be passed quickly in September for the 2020 budget year. It is necessary to pass an immediate budget to meet current demands of the economy and the implications of the coronavirus and – upon receipt of the information on economic behavior and the pandemic in the winter – pass a second budget. Participating in the discussion are the Minister of Defense and Alternate Prime Minister, the Finance Minister, the Economy and Industry Minister, the head of the National Economic Council, the Accountant General and other officials.
