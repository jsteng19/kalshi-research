Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, Finance Minister Moshe Kahlon and Interior Minister Aryeh Deri, have decided on a NIS 3 million compensation package for restaurateurs in Sderot and the area adjacent to the Gaza Strip in order to develop and strengthen the economic resilience of the area. The package will provide compensation worth tens of thousands of shekels to food sector business owners in the area.
