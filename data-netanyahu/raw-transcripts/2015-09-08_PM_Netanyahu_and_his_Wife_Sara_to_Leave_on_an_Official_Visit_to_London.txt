Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara will, tomorrow morning, leave on an official visit to London. The Prime Minister will meet with British Prime Minister David Cameron. The two men will discuss diplomatic, strategic and bilateral issues, as well as joint challenges in the fields of security, economics and cyber. Prime Minister Netanyahu will also meet with MPs and Jewish community leaders.

The two prime ministers last met during British Prime Minister Cameron's visit to Israel in March 2014.
