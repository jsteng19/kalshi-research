Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, chaired discussions with government ministers, ministry director generals, and senior medical and scientific officials, at which various plans for easing the restrictions were presented. Prime Minister Netanyahu noted in the preliminary discussions that the plan has to be gradual and responsible. The relevant officials presented the strategy that has been formulated following lengthy talks and ramified staff work of all government ministries and expert teams. The National Security Council (NSC) has held a dialogue with many countries in order to study various challenges and proposals. The Prime Minister and ministers were presented with the alternatives that were raised in the discussions.
