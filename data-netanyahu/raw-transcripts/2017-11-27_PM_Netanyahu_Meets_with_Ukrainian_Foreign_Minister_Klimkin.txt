Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, in Jerusalem, met with Ukrainian Foreign Minister Pavlo Klimkin. The two discussed deepening bilateral cooperation in – inter alia – technology, cyber and agriculture.

The Prime Minister reviewed regional threats, especially the Iranian threat, and said, "Israel takes the Iranian threats seriously; this is the lesson of our history." Ministers Tzachi Hanegbi and Ze'ev Elkin, and Deputy Minister Michael Oren, also participated in the meeting.
