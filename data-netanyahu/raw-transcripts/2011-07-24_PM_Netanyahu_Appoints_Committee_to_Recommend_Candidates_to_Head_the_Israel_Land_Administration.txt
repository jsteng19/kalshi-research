Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today appointed a committee to recommend to him, Finance Minister Yuval Steinitz and Construction and Housing Minister Ariel Atias, candidates to head the Israel Land administration.

The members of the committee are: Prime Minister's Office Director-General Eyal Gabai (chairman), Finance Ministry Budget Director Dr. Udi Nisan and Construction and Housing Ministry Director-General Mordechai Mordechai.

In his letter of appointment, Prime Minister Netanyahu said that the committee must act will all possible speed on the appointment of a candidate who could lead the reform and deal with the unique challenges facing the ILA.

Due to the both foregoing and his various other tasks, PMO Dir.-Gen. Gabai will stay in office for several more weeks, at the Prime Minister's request.
