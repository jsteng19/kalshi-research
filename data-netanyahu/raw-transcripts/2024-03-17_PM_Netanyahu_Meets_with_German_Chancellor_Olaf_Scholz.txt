Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with German Chancellor Olaf Scholz.

Also participating in the meeting were:

For the Israeli side – the Strategic Affairs Minister, the Director of the National Security Council, the Prime Minister's Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Prime Minister's Foreign Policy Adviser and the Israeli Ambassador to German.

For the German side – the National Security Adviser, the Chief-of-Staff in the Chancellor's Office, the Adviser on Middle Eastern Affairs and the German Ambassador to Israel.

Following the meeting, the leaders issued statements to the media.
