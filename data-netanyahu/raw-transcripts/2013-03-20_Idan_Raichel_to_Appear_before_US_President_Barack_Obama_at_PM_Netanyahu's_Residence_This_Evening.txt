Idan Raichel will appear after the dinner at Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's official residence. Raichel will briefly present his Project and will sing "Mima'amakim" together with Kabra Kasai, who will sing in Amharic; Maya Avraham will provide background vocals. Raichel will also sing "Gift of Acceptance" (click here for lyrics), which he wrote for India Arie. She and Raichel sang the song before President Obama on Martin Luther King Day in Washington, on 14 January 2010.
