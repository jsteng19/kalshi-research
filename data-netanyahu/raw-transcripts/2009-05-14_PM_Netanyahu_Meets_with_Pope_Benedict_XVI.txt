Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this afternoon in the

Basilica of the Annunciation

Nazareth

, met with Pope Benedict XVI.

Prime Minister Netanyahu asked the Pontiff to take action and make his voice heard, in a clear and unequivocal fashion, against anti-Semitism in the world, and emphasized the Iranian President's anti-Semitic policy, as well as his call for the destruction of the State of Israel.

The two leaders discussed their desire to advance both the

diplomatic relations between Israel and the Vatican

and the dialogue and understanding between Judaism and Christianity.

The meeting was characterized by a good atmosphere and continued longer than planned.
