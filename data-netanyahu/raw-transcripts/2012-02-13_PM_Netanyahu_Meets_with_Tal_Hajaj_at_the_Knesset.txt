Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, at the Knesset met with 14-year-old Tal Hajaj who told him about the difficulties her family has experienced since her father came to the State of Israel after having served in the South Lebanon Army.

Minister Yossi Peled, who is responsible for the welfare of SLA families, also attended the meeting.

Prime Minister Netanyahu asked Minister Peled to consider how the family's situation might be improved and told Tal, "I ask you to please return home, to your studies, and to stop the hunger strike."

Tal said that she would do so and thanked the Prime Minister for his time.
