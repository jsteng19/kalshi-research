Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-italy110423

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, spoke with Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni and conveyed condolences on behalf of all citizens of Israel over the death of Alessandro Parini, who was murdered in the terrorist attack in Tel Aviv, in which two of his friends were wounded.

The leaders made it clear that Israel and Italy would continue to cooperate in their determined and unrelenting fight against terrorism.
