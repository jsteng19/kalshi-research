Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke with Greek Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras and Cypriot President Nicos Anastasiades. Among the issues discussed were advancing the feasibility study for laying a gas pipeline from Israel to Cyprus and from there to Greece and Western Europe.

Cypriot President Anastasiades and Greek Prime Minister Tsipras wished Prime Minister Netanyahu a happy holiday. The leaders noted that they are looking forward to the trilateral summit next month.
