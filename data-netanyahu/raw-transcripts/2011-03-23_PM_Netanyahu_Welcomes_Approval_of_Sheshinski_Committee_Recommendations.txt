Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today commended the members of

the Knesset Finance Committee

for approving

the Sheshinski Committee recommendations

. He said that this was an important step that would move the State of Israel and its citizens forward towards the development of independent energy sources. "This is important news for Israelis, who will enjoy not only clean energy but royalties that will benefit education and security," the Prime Minister said.
