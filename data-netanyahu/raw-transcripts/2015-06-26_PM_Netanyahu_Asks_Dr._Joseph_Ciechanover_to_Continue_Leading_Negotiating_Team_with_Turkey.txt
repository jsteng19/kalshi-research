Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Dr. Joseph Ciechanover and asked him to continue leading the negotiating team with Turkey so that he might evaluate ways to provide for the various aspects of bilateral relations.

The Prime Minister said that he appreciates the work that has been done up until now by the members of the team – National Security Adviser Yossi Coen and Dr. Ciechanover – and notes they have met over the last year with the Turkish team and will continue to do so on issues entrusted to them by the Prime Minister
