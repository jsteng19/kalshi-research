Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, met with Brazilian Foreign Minister Aloysio Nunes. The two discussed deepening bilateral cooperation in – inter alia – security, education, culture and academia.

Prime Minister Netanyahu expressed desire to enhance bilateral cooperation and said that Israel is greatly interested in ties with Brazil and believes in their latent potential.

Brazilian Foreign Minister Nunes invited Prime Minister Netanyahu to visit Brazil.

At the start of the meeting, the two countries' ambassadors signed a social insurance agreement that provides for the status of social rights (including pensions) for Israeli and Brazilian nationals while they are in the other country.
