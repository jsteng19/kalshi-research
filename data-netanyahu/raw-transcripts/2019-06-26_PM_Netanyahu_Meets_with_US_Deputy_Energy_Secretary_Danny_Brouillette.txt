Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, at Tel Aviv University Cyber Week, met with US Deputy Energy Secretary Danny Brouillette. Energy Minister Dr. Yuval Steinitz and National Security Adviser and National Security Council Chairman Meir Ben-Shabbat also attended the meeting. Among the issues discussed were the economic pressure on Iran and the strengthening of bilateral cooperation in cyber defense and energy infrastructures.
