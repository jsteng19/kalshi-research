Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has decided to appoint Moshik Aviv as Head of the National Public Diplomacy Directorate.

Moshe Aviv has, over the past decade, served in various senior civil service positions including Director of the Information Center and Head of the National Events and Ceremonies Authority for the past eight years.

He previously commanded the IDF College for three years, served as Head of the IDF Culture Branch for four years and headed the IDF Education and Social Integration Branch for two years.

His appointment will be submitted for Government approval on Sunday.
