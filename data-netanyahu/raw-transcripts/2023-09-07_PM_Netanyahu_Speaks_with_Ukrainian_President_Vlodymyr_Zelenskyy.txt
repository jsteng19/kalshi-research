Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. The two leaders discussed the continuation of Israeli assistance to Ukraine, including to Ukrainian refugees in Israel, as well as the advancement of development assistance of civilian air defense systems.

Prime Minister Netanyahu also raised the need to do everything possible to ensure the arrival of Jews to Uman this year in order to facilitate freedom of worship.
