Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-archi210125

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, spoke with IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Herzi Halevi, and thanked him for his many years of service as well as for commanding the IDF in the War of Redemption on seven fronts, which has led to major achievements for the State of Israel.

The two agreed to meet in the coming days.
