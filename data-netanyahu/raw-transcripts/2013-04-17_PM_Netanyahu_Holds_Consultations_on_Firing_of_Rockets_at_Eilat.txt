Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, who is in London, for the funeral of the late Margaret Thatcher, spoke a short while ago with Defense Minister Moshe Yaalon and Eilat Mayor Yitzhak Halevy on the firing of rockets at Eilat. Earlier, the Prime Minister held security consultations in order to discuss how to respond to the firing.
