PM Netanyahu to Leave for Germany and Meeting with Chancellor Angela Merkel; meets with Icelandic & Norwegian Foreign Ministers and with US UN Ambassador Samantha Power

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu will leave shortly for Germany where he will meet with German Chancellor Angela Merkel in the framework of a G2G meeting between the Israeli and German governments. Prime Minister Netanyahu, this morning met with Icelandic Foreign Minister Grunnar Bragi Sveinsson. They discussed strengthening bilateral relations and increasing cooperation in the spheres of knowledge and technology.
