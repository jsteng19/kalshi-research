Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, spoke with Bahraini Crown Prince Salman bin Hamad Al Khalifa. The Crown Prince reiterated his invitation for the Prime Minister to visit Bahrain; the leaders determined that at the first opportunity that the morbidity will permit, Prime Minister Netanyahu will visit Bahrain.

The Bahraini Crown Prince noted that he was interested in examining the possibility of Bahrain joining the initiative to produce vaccines that is being planned to be established in Israel together with other countries.
