Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu strongly condemns the murderous terror attack in London and sends his condolences to the victims and wishes for a speedy recovery to the wounded.

Prime Minister Netanyahu notes, "The citizens of Israel were among the first to face the challenge of vehicular ramming and stabbing attacks. We must stand shoulder-to-shoulder with the citizens of Britain and the entire civilized world against radical Islamic terrorism."
