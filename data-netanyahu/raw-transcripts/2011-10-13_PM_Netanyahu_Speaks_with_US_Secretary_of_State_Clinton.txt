US Secretary of State Hilary Clinton phoned Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today congratulated him on achieving the agreement for the return of captive soldier Gilad Shalit and added that courage and leadership were necessary to make such decisions.Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked US Secy. Of State Clinton and said that this was a difficult decision for him but added that leadership is tested at such moments.
