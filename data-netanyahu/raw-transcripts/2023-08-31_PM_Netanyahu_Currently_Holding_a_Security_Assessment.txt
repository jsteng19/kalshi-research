Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding a security assessment at the Kirya in Tel Aviv.

Attending the assessment are the Defense Minister, the National Security Minister, the Director of the National Security Council, the Director of the ISA, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Inspector General of the Israel Police, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Head of the IDF Intelligence Branch, the Head of the IDF Personnel Branch, the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories and the Head of Research at the IDF Intelligence Branch.
