Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening met with special US Middle East envoy George Mitchell. The meeting lasted for four hours, including two hours privately. The meeting was friendly, positive and encompassed the full range of issues on the agenda. The Prime Minister said: "Israel is working to advance peace and security with our Palestinian neighbors and with the wider Arab world." Envoy Mitchell emphasized that the US and Israel are allies and added that the link between them was unbreakable.
