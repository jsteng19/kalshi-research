Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening at his Jerusalem residence, held a warm and cordial meeting with former US President Bill Clinton, who has come to Israel for the funeral of Israel's ninth President, Shimon Peres, which will be held tomorrow on Mt. Herzl in Jerusalem. Also attending the meeting were Acting National Security Advisor and Head of the National Security Council Brig.-Gen. (ret.) Jacob Nagel and attorney Yitzhak Molcho.

Former US President Clinton was welcomed by Prime Minister Netanyahu and his wife Sara. The former President signed the guestbook.
