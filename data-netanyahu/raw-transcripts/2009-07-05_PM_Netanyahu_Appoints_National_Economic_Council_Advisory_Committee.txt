Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, decided to appoint the National Economic Council (NEC) Advisory Committee. Former Finance Ministry Budget Director, and current Chairman of Whitewater, Inc., Uri Yogev will chair the Advisory Committee. The other 12 members of the Advisory Committee are experts and senior personnel with vast experience in a variety of areas such as academia, society and industry, and are from both the public and private sectors.

The Advisory Committee will advise the Prime Minister and the NEC itself and assist them in formulating policy recommendations.

Prof. Eugene Kandel will chair the NEC.
