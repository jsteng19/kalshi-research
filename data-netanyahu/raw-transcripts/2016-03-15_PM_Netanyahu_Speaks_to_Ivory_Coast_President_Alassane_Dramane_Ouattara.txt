Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Ivory Coast President Alassane Dramane Ouattara and expressed his condolences, and the solidarity of the Israeli people, over the terrorist attack that occurred this past Sunday at a resort in Ivory Coast. The Prime Minister said that Israel is ready to assist in the common fight against terrorism.

Prime Minister Netanyahu invited President Ouattara to visit Israel.

Ivory Coast President Ouattara thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu for his condolences and for his support and willingness to assist in the fight against terrorism.
