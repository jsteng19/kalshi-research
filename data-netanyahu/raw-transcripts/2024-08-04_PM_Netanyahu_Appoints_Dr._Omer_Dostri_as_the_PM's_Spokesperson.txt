Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has decided to appoint Dr. Omer Dostri as the Prime Minister's Spokesperson. Dr. Dostri (37), has considerable experience in the media, and holds a Ph.D. in political science from Bar-Ilan University. He has served as a researcher in research institutes and has published numerous articles on the military and national security. Prime Minister approved the appointment and congratulated Dr. Dostri on leading the communications division in the Prime Minister's Office.
