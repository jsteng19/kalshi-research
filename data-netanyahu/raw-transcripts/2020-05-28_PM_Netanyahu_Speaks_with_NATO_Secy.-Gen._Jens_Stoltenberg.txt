Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke today with NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg, who called to congratulate the Prime Minister on the establishment of his new government. The two discussed regional challenges and widening the joint cooperation between Israel and the NATO alliance. NATO Secy.-Gen. Stoltenberg said that the alliance views Israel as an important partner in safeguarding security in the Euro-Atlantic arena and invited Prime Minister Netanyahu to visit NATO headquarters. The Prime Minister invited the Secretary General to visit Israel.
