Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu telephoned Rafi Biton, the father of the baby girl Adel who was critically injured in a 14 March terrorist attack near Ariel.The Prime Minister updated Biton on the arrest of the suspects involved and wished him and his family a happy Passover.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "I, Sara and the entire Jewish People are praying for the wellbeing of our sweet little girl."
