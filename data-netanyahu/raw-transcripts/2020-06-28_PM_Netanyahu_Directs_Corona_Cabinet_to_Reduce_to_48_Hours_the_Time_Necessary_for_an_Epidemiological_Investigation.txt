Prime Minister Netanyahu directed the Corona Cabinet, to reduce to 48 hours the time necessary for an epidemiological investigation from the moment patients are located until all people who have been in contact with them enter quarantine. He also directed that the time from when an application for a test is received until the test is carried out and the results are delivered, be shortened to 12 hours. Also discussed were the possibility of reducing work in the in the public sector so that 30% of work is done at home and increased enforcement. The discussion on reducing gatherings will continue tomorrow.
