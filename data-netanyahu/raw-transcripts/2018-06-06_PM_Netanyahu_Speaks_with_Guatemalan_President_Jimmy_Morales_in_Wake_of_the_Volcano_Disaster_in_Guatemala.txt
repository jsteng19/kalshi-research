Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Guatemalan President Jimmy Morales in wake of the volcano disaster in Guatemala. The Prime Minister conveyed his condolences, as well as those of the Israeli people, and offered all medical and logistical assistance from Israel.

Prime Minister Netanyahu ordered that preparations be made for the immediate departure of a specialist medical team in coordination with the relevant agencies.
