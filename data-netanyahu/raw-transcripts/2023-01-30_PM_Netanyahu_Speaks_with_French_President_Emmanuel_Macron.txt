Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, last night, spoke with French President Emmanuel Macron and thanked him for sharing in the bereavement of the families who lost loved ones in the murderous terrorist attack in Jerusalem. Prime Minister Netanyahu and French President Macron sharply condemned Iran's active participation in harming innocent civilians in Ukraine. The two leaders agreed to meet soon as part of the continual dialogue between Israel and France.
