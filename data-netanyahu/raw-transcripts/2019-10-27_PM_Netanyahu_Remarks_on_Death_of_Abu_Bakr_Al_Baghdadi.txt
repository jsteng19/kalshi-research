Prime Minister Netanyahu made the following remarks on the death of Abu Bakr Al Baghdadi: “I want to congratulate President Trump on the impressive achievement, an operation that led to the death of the Daash leader Al Baghdadi. This reflects the united resolve of the free countries led by the United States to fight the forces of terror, the terror organizations and the terrorist states. This is an important milestone, but it’s part of a longer battle which we must win.”
