Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu eulogized the late Menachem Golan: "Menachem was one of the pillars of Israeli cinema. He had a role in the making of unforgettable productions and expressed the Israeli spirit and the Israeli experience. Menachem Golan will be remembered in Israel as a pioneer in his field and as one who brought the Israeli cinema to the world's attention."
