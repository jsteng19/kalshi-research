Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-uk210125

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, spoke with British Prime Minister Keir Starmer, who thanked him for the release of British citizen Emily Damari, and congratulated him on his actions for the release of the other hostages.

Prime Minister Netanyahu raised the issue of the weapons export licenses to Israel that have been frozen in the UK. Prime Minister Starmer said that an evaluation of the issue is being carried out.
