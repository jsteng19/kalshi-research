Following is the text of the letter that Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu sent this evening to UK Prime Minister David Cameron:

"Dear Prime Minister,

On behalf of the Government of Israel, please accept my heartfelt congratulations on the birth of the Princess.

The joy felt by the British people today is shared by millions of people around the world, including in Israel.

Please convey my warmest wishes to Her Majesty, the proud parents and the entire Royal Family.

Sincerely,

Benjamin Netanyahu"
