Prime Minister Netanyahu, held a consultation with Health Minister Yuli Edelstein, Interior Minister Aryeh Deri, National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat and other officials.

It was decided to allow the opening of houses of worship, from tomorrow (Wednesday, 20 May 2020), according to the following outline:

* Up to 50 people present

* Maintaining two meters' distance between worshippers

* Appointment of a corona official

* Wearing of masks

* Maintaining the rules of hygiene
