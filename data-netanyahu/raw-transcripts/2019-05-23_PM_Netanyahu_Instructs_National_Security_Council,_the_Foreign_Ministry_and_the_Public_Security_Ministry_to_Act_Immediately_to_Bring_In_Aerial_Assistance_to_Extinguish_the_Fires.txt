Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, instructed the National Security Council, the Foreign Ministry and the Public Security Ministry to act immediately to bring in aerial assistance to extinguish the fires; to this end he spoke with Public Security Minister Gilad Erdan and Fire and Rescue Service Commissioner Dedi Simchi. The Prime Minister is continuing to monitor developments and is receiving regular updates.
