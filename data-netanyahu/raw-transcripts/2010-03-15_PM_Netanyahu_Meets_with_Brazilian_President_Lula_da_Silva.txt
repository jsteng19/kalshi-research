Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening met with Brazilian President Luis Ignacio (Lula) Da Silva. The two leaders agreed to hold joint biannual meetings between the two governments, alternatively in Israel and Brazil, as well as annual meetings between leading business people and senior economic official from the two countries. At Brazil's request, an effort will be made to hold the first inter-governmental meeting this year.
