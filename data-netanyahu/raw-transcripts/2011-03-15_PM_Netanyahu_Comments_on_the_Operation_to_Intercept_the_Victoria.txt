Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today during his tour of the south, said that, "Very early this morning, I approved

the Navy's interception of the Victoria

. We had a solid basis that onboard the ship was weaponry destined for use against Israel. Considerable weaponry – which was destined for terrorist forces in the heart of Gaza – was found onboard the vessel. The operation was carried out at sea in accordance with all international rules. The weaponry originated in Iran, which is trying to arm the Gaza Strip."
