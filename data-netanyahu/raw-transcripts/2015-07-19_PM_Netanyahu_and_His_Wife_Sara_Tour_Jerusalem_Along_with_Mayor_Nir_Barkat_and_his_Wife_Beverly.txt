Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara toured Jerusalem, together with Mayor Nir Barkat and his wife Beverly. A map of future Light Rail lines – which will also connect with the fast rail line between Jerusalem and Tel Aviv – was displayed during the tour. During their trip on the Light Rail, Mayor Barkat also presented the business district that will be built at the entrance to the city and create thousands of jobs. .
