Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/event-condolences070624

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, paid a condolence visit to the family of former minister David Levy, of blessed memory, in Beit She'an. The Prime Minister spoke with his widow Rachel and their children, who told him about David, how he was loved by his family as a father and grandfather. Prime Minister Netanyahu conveyed sincere condolences from his wife Sara, who was unable to come, and spoke of his joint work with David, as well as the latter's contribution to the State of Israel.
