Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu held a comprehensive discussion on procurement and acquisition regarding the coronavirus. Following the discussion, the Prime Minister instructed the Director of the Mossad and the Director General of the Defense Ministry – along with Sarel – to establish a command center and be responsible for the necessary procurement.

The national command center will integrate additional elements from the Health, Finance and Justice ministries, along with others, in the procurement efforts. Prime Minister Netanyahu also instructed the security industries to evaluate the possibility of local production.
