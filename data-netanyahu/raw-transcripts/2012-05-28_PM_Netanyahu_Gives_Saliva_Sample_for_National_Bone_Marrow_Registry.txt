Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today gave a saliva sample and joined the national bone marrow donor registry established by the

Ezer Mizion

organization.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked the Ezer Mizion representatives for their important work in saving lives and said, "I call on all Israelis, from all communities, to donate and thereby assist in saving lives. It is said that whoever saves one life, it as if he has saved an entire world. I ask all Israelis to help and to donate; come, let us save worlds."
