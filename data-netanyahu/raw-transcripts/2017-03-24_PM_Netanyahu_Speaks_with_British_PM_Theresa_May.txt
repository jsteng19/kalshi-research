Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke, a short time ago this afternoon, with British Prime Minister Theresa May in wake of Wednesday's terrorist attack in London.

Prime Minister Netanyahu conveyed his condolences to the families of those who were murdered and to the British people and said that the war on terrorism is incumbent on everyone.

British Prime Minister May thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu for the conversation and for the two countries' strengthening ties in the war on terrorism.
