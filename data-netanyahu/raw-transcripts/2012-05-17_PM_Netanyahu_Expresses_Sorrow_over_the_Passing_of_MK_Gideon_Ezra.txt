Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu expresses his deep sorrow over the passing of

MK Gideon Ezra

. The Prime Minister said, "Gideon loyally served the Land of Israel throughout his entire life.

His contribution to the security of the state was considerable; most of it will remain secret. My wife and I send our condolences to his wife and family. May his memory be blessed."
