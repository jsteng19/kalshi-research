Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu in New York, held a series of diplomatic meetings.

The Prime Minister met with Panamanian President Juan Carlos Varela and discussed with him the importance that Israel ascribes to strengthening bilateral cooperation as well as the considerable potential of such cooperation. The two leaders exchanged mutual invitations to visit.

Prime Minister Netanyahu then met with Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe and discussed with him the strengthening of economic cooperation with emphasis on – inter alia – cyber and he opening of direct air links.
