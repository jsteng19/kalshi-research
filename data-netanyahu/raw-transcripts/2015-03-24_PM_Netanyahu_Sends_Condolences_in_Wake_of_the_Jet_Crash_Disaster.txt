PM Netanyahu Sends Condolences in Wake of the Jet Crash Disaster

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu on behalf of the people of Israel, sent condolences to French President Francois Hollande regarding the tragedy that occurred in his country.

Prime Minister Netanyahu also spoke with German Chancellor Angela Merkel and Spanish Prime Minister Mariano Rajoy in wake of the awful tragedy.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "At this difficult time, I embrace the family of the Israeli citizen, Eyal Baum, and send condolences to the families of the passengers from Germany, Spain, Turkey and Belgium who lost their dear ones. Israel is prepared to render such assistance as may be requested."
