Prime Minister Netanyahu, at the start of the weekly Cabinet meeting, made the following remarks regarding the terrorist attack near Ariel: "We are in pursuit of the terrorists at two points in the Ariel area. I offer my support to the soldiers of the IDF, the ISA and the security forces who are pursuing the terrorists. I am certain that they will apprehend them and we will deal with them to the fullest extent of the law as we have done in all of the recent incidents."
