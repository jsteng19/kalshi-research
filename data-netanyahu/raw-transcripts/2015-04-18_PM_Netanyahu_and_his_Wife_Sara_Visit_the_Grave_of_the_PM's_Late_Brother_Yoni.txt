Ahead of Remembrance Day for the Fallen of Israel's Wars, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara, this evening, at the Mt. Herzl military cemetery in Jerusalem, visited the grave of the former's brother Yoni, who fell during the operation to free the hostages at Entebbe in 1976. The Netanyahus visited the grave this evening in order to avoid bothering the bereaved families.
