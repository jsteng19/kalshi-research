Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Yohanan Danino regarding Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch's to extend Commissioner Danino's term by an additional year. The Prime Minister commended Commissioner Danino and noted his contribution to moving the Israel Police forward. He asked him to convey Israelis' appreciation to all Israel Police personnel for their effort to defeat crime and increase personal security.

Prime Minister Netanyahu also spoke to Prison Service Commissioner Lt.-Gen. Aharon Franko, whose term was similarly extended by an additional year. The Prime Minister said that the Prison Service has taken on additional tasks in recent years such as detaining illegal migrants and asked to convey his thanks to Prison Service personnel for their dedication.
