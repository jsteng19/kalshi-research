PM Netanyahu received the report and recommendations formulated by former minister Moshe Nissim on conversion. Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked former minister Nissim for his intensive work on this important issue. The Prime Minister and his staff will review the report.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "We are continuing our constant efforts to find solutions that strengthen unity among the Jewish People in Israel and the Diaspora."
