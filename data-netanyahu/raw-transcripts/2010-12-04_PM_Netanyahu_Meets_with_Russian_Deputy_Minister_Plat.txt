Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu met a short while ago today with Russian Deputy Civil Defense, Emergency and Disaster Relief Minister Pavel Plat, who is responsible for the Russian delegation that has arrived in Israel. The Prime Minister thanked him for Russia's effort in helping to extinguish the massive Carmel brushfire and said that the State of Israel very much appreciates the significant assistance that Russia is currently extending to it.
