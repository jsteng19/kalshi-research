Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, in Athens, met with Cypriot President Nicos Anastasiades. The two discussed strengthening bilateral relations in various fields including – inter alia – energy, public security, the economy and tourism. Energy Minister Yuval Steinitz and Cypriot Energy, Commerce, Industry and Tourism Minister George Lakkotrypis also attended the meeting. Prime Minister Netanyahu, Cypriot President Anastasiades and Greek Prime Minister Kyriakos Mitsotakis are due to hold the seventh trilateral Israel-Greece-Cyprus summit during which the East Med gas pipeline agreement will be signed.
