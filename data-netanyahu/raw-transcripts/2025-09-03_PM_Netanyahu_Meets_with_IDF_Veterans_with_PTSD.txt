Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, met with the forum of fighters suffering from PTSD, which is coordinated by Itzik Saidyan, who survived the APC tragedy in Operation Protective Edge.

The forum members told the Prime Minister about their complicated daily coping and their individual rehabilitation processes.

Prime Minister Netanyahu stood by the fighters, expressed his appreciation and thanked them for their contributions to the security of Israel.

The Prime Minister instructed Prime Minister's Office Acting Director General Drorit Steinmetz to evaluate additional assistance for those suffering from PTSD so that they will be able to better utilize their rights.
