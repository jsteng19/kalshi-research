Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, made the following remarks: "The terrorist attack against the Jewish community in Halle Germany on Yom Kippur, the holiest day of our people, is additional testimony that anti-semitism in Europe is increasing. On behalf of the people of Israel, I send condolences to the families of the victims and wishes for a quick recovery to the injured. I call on the German authorities to continue taking determined action against anti-semitism."
