Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, has just spoken with US President Joe Biden.

The Prime Minister discussed with the American President the progress in the negotiations to release our hostages and updated him on the mandate he gave to the negotiating team to Doha in order to advance the release of our hostages.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked President Biden and President-elect Donald Trump for cooperating in this sacred mission.
