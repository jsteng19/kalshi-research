Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Health Minister Yaakov Litzman have appointed Prof. Nimrod Maimon to head the Health Ministry Magen Avot v'Imahot ("Fathers and Mothers Shield") program. The program was formulated by Prof. Ronni Gamzu and coordinates issues dealing with retirement homes. Prof. Nimrod Maimon has been the director of the Internal Medicine B Department at Soroka Medical Center for the past eight years, having previously served as the hospital's deputy director. Recently, his department served as the Corona B Respiratory Department, in which patients with corona symptoms were hospitalized.
