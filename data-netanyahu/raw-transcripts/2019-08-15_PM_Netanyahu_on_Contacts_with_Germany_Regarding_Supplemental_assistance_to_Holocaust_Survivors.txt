Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu: “We have been holding contacts with the German government on giving supplemental assistance to Holocaust survivors; this is in addition to the assistance supplements that we are giving. The German government has just informed us that they will be giving a supplement to thousands of Holocaust survivors: Hundreds of Euro per month. This is important. These people deserve it. I thank the German government.”
