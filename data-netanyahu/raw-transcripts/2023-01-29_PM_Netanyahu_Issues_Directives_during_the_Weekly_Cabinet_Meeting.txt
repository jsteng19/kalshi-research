Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, in the course of the weekly Cabinet meeting today , issued the following directives: 1. The Israeli Security Agency (ISA) will submit an immediate opinion on carrying out additional deterrent measures regarding the families of terrorists that express support for terrorism. 2. The Acting Interior Minister and the Attorney General will submit draft legislation on revoking residency and citizenship of terrorists and removing them to the territory of the Palestinian Authority. 3. The Labor Minister and the Attorney General will prepare a draft memorandum on legislation that would allow for the immediate dismissal of workers who have supported terrorism, without need for a hearing. 4. The Government supports exempting from tabling [in the Knesset] any draft legislation dealing with the foregoing issues, of strengthening deterrence by means of taking action against terrorists' immediate circle.
