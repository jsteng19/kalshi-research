Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with former Austrian Chancellor Sebastian Kurz. Prime Minister Netanyahu: "I am delighted to see you again, Sebastian. You have been a tremendous friend of the State of Israel, a champion of fighting antisemitism, a great leader for Austria. It’s good to see you again, back in Jerusalem. You are warmly welcomed as a friend." Former Austrian Chancellor Kurz: "It’s always good to be here. Thank you for taking time, for giving us an overview about Israel and the region, and the challenges you have in the region."
