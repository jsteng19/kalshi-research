Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu:

"On behalf of all citizens of Israel, my wife and I send heartfelt condolences to the families of Sgt. Yair Eliyahu and Sgt. Asaf Zamir, who fell in combat in the Gaza Strip.

The whole nation grieves over the loss of our soldiers who fought courageously in the campaign to defeat Hamas and release our hostages.

The entire people embrace the dear families at this time of deep sorrow.

May the memory of our heroes be blessed and enshrined in our hearts forever."
