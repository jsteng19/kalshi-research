Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke with Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The two leaders agreed to continue and advance bilateral cooperation in the fight against the coronavirus with emphasis on technological cooperation and tests. The two also agreed to hold a digital conference on innovation in the field of solar energy in order to jointly achieve more inexpensive solutions that will benefit the economies of both countries and the entire world.
