Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Finance Minister Yisrael Katz, in coordination with Labor, Social Affairs and Social Services Minister Itzik Shmuli, have agreed to allocate NIS 10 million to the Labor, Social Affairs and Social Services Ministry for shelters for women who are victims of violence and their children. The budget was approved by the Cabinet as part of the most recently approved economic plan. Among the provisions of the agreement are the following: Each of the 14 shelters will receive a one-time payment of NIS 300,000. One thousand computers will be purchased for children in the shelters and children who have been exposed to violence in the community. 24/7 centers will be refurbished ahead of their opening. Equipment will be procured for apartments of men who have been ordered to move out of their homes.
