Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, spoke by telephone with NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg, thanked him for the invitation for Israel to open a mission at NATO headquarters in Brussels and said that Israel accepts the invitation.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that Israel and NATO would increase cooperation against both terrorist elements in the Middle East and common threats. He invited NATO Secy.-Gen. Stoltenberg to visit Israel.
