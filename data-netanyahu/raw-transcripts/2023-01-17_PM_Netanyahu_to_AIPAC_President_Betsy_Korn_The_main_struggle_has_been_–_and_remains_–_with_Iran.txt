Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with AIPAC President Betsy Korn, AIPAC CEO Howard Kohr and AIPAC-Israel Director Cameron Brown. Prime Minister Netanyahu emphasized that the main struggle has been – and remains – with Iran, and thanked the senior AIPAC officials for their contribution to strengthening the bond between the US and Israel.
