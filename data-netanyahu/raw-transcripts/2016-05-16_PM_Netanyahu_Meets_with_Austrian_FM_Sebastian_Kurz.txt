Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today (Monday, 16 May 2016), met with Austrian Foreign Minister Sebastian Kurz. The Prime Minister stressed the importance of direct negotiations with the Palestinians and noted the threat of radical Islam and its implications for Israel and Europe alike.

The two also signed a work and holiday visa agreement and a memorandum of understanding on education and cultural issues.
