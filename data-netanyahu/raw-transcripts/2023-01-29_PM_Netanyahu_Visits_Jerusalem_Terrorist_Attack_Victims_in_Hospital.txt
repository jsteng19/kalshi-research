Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/event-hospital290123

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, at the Shaare Zedek and Hadassah-Mt. Scopus hospitals, visited those who were wounded in the Jerusalem terrorist attacks. Prime Minister Netanyahu spoke with the wounded and their families and said that he stood by them. He heard from them details about the events and stressed that he would work to increase security. Prime Minister Netanyahu was also updated by the medical teams on the condition of the wounded and the care they are receiving.
