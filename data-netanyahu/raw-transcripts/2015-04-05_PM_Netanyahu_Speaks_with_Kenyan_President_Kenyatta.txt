Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spokekenya050415



Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Kenyan President Uhuru Kenyatta and conveyed his condolences – on behalf of the citizens of Israel – in wake of the reprehensible terrorist attack that was perpetrated in Kenya.

Prime Minister Netanyahu told Kenyan President Kenyatta that Israel and Kenya were together in the war against terrorism and offered such assistance as may be requested.
