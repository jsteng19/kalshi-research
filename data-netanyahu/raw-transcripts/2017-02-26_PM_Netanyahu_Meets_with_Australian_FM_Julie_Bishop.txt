Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this morning met with Australian Foreign Minister Julie Bishop. He explained the substantial dangers of the nuclear agreement and in Iran's aggressive conduct in the region as well as in its support for terrorism and the continued development of its ballistic missile program. The Prime Minister also explained the difference between the concept of two states and the content and substance of two states.

It was agreed to upgrade security, intelligence, economic, cyber and technology relations.
