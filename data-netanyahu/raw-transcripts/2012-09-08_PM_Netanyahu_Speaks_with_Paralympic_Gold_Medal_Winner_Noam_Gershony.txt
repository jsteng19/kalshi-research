Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening spoke with tennis player

Noam Gershony

, who won a gold medal at the Paralympics in London and told him, "I was very excited about your victory. The State of Israel embraces you for your great achievement. You symbolize the victory of the human spirit over the difficulties created by the reality in which we live. This is gold for you and the country."
