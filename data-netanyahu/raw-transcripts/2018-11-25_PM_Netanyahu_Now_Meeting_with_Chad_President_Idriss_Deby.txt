Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding a private meeting with the President of the Republic of Chad, Idriss Deby, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem.

Prime Minister Netanyahu welcomed Chad President Deby to Israel.

The two will deliver statements to the media following their meeting and will meet again later this evening over dinner at the Prime Minister's Residence in Jerusalem.
