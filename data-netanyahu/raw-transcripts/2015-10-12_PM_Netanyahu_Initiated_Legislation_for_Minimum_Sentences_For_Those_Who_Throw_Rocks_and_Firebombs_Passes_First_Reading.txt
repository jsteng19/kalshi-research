The Knesset approved in its first reading legislation introduced by Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu for minimum prison sentences for those who throw rocks and firebombs and all other harmful objects.

Afterwards a vote will be held on draft legislation proposing fines on minors who throw rocks and firebombs and their parents.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said:

"This legislation which I initiated is one of the measures adopted by the Cabinet in the framework of the fight against terrorism. There will be additional measures.
