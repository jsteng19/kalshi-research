Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke this afternoon with British Prime Minister Theresa May. The Prime Minister wished Prime Minister May luck as she begins her premiership.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and Prime Minister May discussed ways in which the two countries can maintain and increase cooperation in a wide variety of fields. In addition, the two discussed regional issues and the threat of global terrorism facing the free world.
