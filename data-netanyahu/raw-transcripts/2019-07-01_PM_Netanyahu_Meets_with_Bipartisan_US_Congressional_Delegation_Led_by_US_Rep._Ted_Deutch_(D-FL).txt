Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with a bipartisan US Congressional delegation led by US Rep. Ted Deutch (D-FL). The Prime Minister spoke with the delegation members about the activities of Iran, which is undermining stability in the region and endangering the security of Israel, and ways to increase the pressure on Iran. Prime Minister Netanyahu commended the bipartisan presence expected at the American Independence Day ceremony, which will be held in Jerusalem for the first time.
