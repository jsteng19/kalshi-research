Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening expressed deep sorrow over the passing of

Al Schwimmer, one of the founders of the Israel Air Force and Israel Aircraft Industries

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that, "During his life, Schwimmer contributed greatly to the security of the State of Israel and to the success and development of the IAF. Thanks to him, IAI became a world leader. He was a true Zionist to whom the Jewish People owe a major debt."
