Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today met in Jerusalem with German Foreign Minister Guido Westerwelle. The two men discussed the challenges anticipated for later this month, the tension with Turkey and the Iranian threat, and also stressed the friendship between Israel and Germany. The Prime Minister said that, "I ascribe great importance to direct negotiations in advancing the peace with the Palestinians. Peace will be achieved only through direct negotiations and not through unilateral moves."
