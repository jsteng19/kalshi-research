Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, yesterday (Friday, 22 August 2014), spoke with Shaar Hanegev Regional Council Chairman Alon Schuster and told him, "On behalf of the citizens of Israel, I express my deep sorrow and send my condolences to the family of Daniel Tregeman.

At this difficult time, I would like to support the citizens of Israel. Hamas will pay a heavy price for this terrorist attack. The IDF and the ISA will step up actions against Hamas and the other terrorist organizations in Gaza until the goal of Operation Protective Edge is met."
