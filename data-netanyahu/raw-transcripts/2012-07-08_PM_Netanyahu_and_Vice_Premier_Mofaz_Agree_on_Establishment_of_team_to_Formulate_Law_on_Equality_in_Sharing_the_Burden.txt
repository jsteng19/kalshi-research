Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Vice Premier Shaul Mofaz, today, have agreed on the establishment of a team to formulate the law on equality in sharing the burden and to submit its draft at the next Cabinet meeting.

The team will be composed of Vice Premier Mofaz, Strategic Affairs Minister Moshe Yaalon and MK Yohanan Plesner.

Defense, Finance and Justice Ministry representatives, as well as representatives from the Civilian Service, will participate, as needed, in the team's discussions.
