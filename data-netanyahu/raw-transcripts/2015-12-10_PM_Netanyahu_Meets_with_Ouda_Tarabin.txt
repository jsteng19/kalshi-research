Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today in his Jerusalem office, met with Ouda Tarabin following the latter's return to Israel, and told him, "Welcome home. We had long talks with the Egyptians for years and we are happy to see you with us now. I said that we would bring you back here and it has happened. Now you may start over."

Ouda Tarabin thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu for the actions that were done for his return and said that this was a dream for him.
