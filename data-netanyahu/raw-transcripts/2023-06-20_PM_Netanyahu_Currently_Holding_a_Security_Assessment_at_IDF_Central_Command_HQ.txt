Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding a security assessment at IDF Central Command headquarters.

Participating in the discussion are the Defense Minister, the Strategic Affairs Minister, the Director of the ISA, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Head of the IDF Intelligence Branch, the Head of the IDF Operations Branch, the Head of IDF Central Command and the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories.
