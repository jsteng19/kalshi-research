Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara toured the Taj Mahal.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "This is a moment of relaxation on a very intensive visit. I would like to thank Indian Prime Minister Modi for allowing us this moment as well. In India we have met much love, love for Israel, and here we are in the temple of love."

Prime Minister Netanyahu – at the Taj Mahal – met with Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Sri Yogi Adityanath.
