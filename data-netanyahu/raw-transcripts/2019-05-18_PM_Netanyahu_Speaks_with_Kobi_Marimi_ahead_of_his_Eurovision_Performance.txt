Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke by telephone with Kobi Marimi ahead of his Eurovision performance and wished him success. Following is an excerpt from the Prime Minister's remarks: "Millions are now watching you. Know that an entire people is supporting you. It is important that you believe in yourself Kobi. Do the best that you can; this is what you expect from yourself and this is what we expect from you. We all embrace you and adopt you. I will also be watching you. Good luck, for you and for our country." Kobi Marimi thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu and said that expects to represent the country with honor at tonight's performance.
