Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-etzion100725



Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with the head of the Gush Etzion Regional Council, Yaron Rosenthal.

The Prime Minister expressed his support for the Council head and the residents, and asked to convey his condolences, on behalf of his wife and him, to the family of the man murdered in the brutal terrorist shooting, as well as his wishes for a speedy recovery to the injured.

The Prime Minister praised the citizens and the security forces personnel who acted very resourcefully in eliminating the vile terrorists, saying that we will not allow terrorism in Judea and Samaria to rear its head.
