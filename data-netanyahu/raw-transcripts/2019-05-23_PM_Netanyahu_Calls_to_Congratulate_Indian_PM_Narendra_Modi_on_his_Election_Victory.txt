Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, called to congratulate Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi on his election victory. Following is an excerpt from his remarks:

"Narendra, my friend, congratulations! What an enormous victory! I hope that we can see each other very soon, as soon as you form a government and as soon as we form a government. There is much to discuss on so many other things. Thank you for your congratulations on my victory, but there is one difference: You don’t need a coalition, I do. It’s an amazing result, and it’s a testament to your leadership, and I wish you the greatest success."
