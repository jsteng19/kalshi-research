Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, at his request, met with Supreme Court President Esther Hayut and her deputy Hanan Melcer. The three noted the importance of a substantive and respectful dialogue between the authorities. Supreme Court President Hayut and Deputy President Melcer stressed the importance of maintaining the independence of the judicial authority. Prime Minister Netanyahu emphasized the need for balance between the authorities while holding the aforesaid dialogue.
