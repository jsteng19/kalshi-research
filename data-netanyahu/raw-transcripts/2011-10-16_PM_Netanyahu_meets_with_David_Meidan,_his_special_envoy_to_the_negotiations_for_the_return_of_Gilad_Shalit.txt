Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu met this afternoon with David Meidan, his special envoy to the negotiations for the return of captured soldier Gilad Shalit. Meidan updated the Prime Minister about the agreements reached in discussions he held in Cairo over the weekend regarding the return of Shalit to Israel in the next few days.

Major General Yohanan Locker, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary who was a member of the negotiating team, also participated in the meeting. The Prime Minister told Meidan: "The mission will be accomplished when Gilad Shalit will be home with his family, alive, healthy and well."
