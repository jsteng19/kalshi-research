Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with AIPAC leaders and thanked them for their deep and consistent support of the State of Israel.

Participating in the meeting were the Director of the National Security Council, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the Prime Ministers Foreign Policy Adviser and the Prime Minister's Spokesperson. Among those participating for AIPAC were CEO Howard Kohr, President Michael Tuchin and AIPAC Israel Director Cameron Brown.
