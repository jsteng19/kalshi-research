Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, in his Jerusalem office, hosted a ceremony in which he awarded certificates of appreciation to outstanding National Security Council (NSC) employees. NSC Chairman Yossi Cohen, and NSC employees and their families, also attended the ceremony.

Prime Minister Netanyahu commended the outstanding NSC employees and stressed the importance of the NSC in the decision-making processes of the Prime Minister and government ministers. NSC Chairman Cohen thanked the Prime Minister for his remarks and noted the dedication of the employees who work day and night to assist him and other ministers in dealing with the challenges facing the State of Israel.
