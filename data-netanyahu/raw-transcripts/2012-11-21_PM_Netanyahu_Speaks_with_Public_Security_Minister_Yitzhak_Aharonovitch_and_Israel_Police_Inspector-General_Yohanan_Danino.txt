Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch and with Israel Police Inspector-General Yohanan Danino and asked them to convey his gratitude and that of the Israeli people to the personnel of the Israel Police for working around the clock in order to defend the country. The Prime Minister will meet with the Israel Police senior command tomorrow.
