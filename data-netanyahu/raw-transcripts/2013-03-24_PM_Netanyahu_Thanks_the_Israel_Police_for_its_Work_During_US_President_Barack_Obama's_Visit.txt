Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today met with Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch, Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Yohanan Danino and Jerusalem District Police Commander Yossi Priyanti and thanked them for their dedicated and professional work during Operation Unbreakable Alliance, US President Barack Obama's visit to Israel.

Prime Minister Netanyahu gave Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Danino a basketball in the colors of the Israeli and American flags on which he signed "To the Israel Police, thank you for your excellent work during US President Obama's visit." The Prime Minister also said, "I thank you all for your splendid work. You maintained security, order and our honor."

Prime Minister Netanyahu wished all Israel Police officers and volunteers and their families a Happy Passover.

Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Danino thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu and said, "I would like to thank Israel Police officers and volunteers and the many agencies that worked together on a mission we defined as national. We are pleased that it passed peacefully."
