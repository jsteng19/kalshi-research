Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today met with a group of Project Masa high school students who told him about the strength of their Project Masa experience, which has taken them throughout the country, including Jerusalem. The Prime Minister said, "The story of the Jewish People, of Zionism and our unique link to the Land of Israel, finds expression in the heritage sites that you are visiting in the course of your Masa experience. I am pleased to hear about its success. There is great importance in our linking up with the heritage of the Jewish People in its Land. This link to our past is very important in assuring our future."
