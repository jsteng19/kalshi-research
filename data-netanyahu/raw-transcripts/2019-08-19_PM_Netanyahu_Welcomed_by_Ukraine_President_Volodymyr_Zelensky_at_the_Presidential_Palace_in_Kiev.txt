Prime Minister Netanyahu, at the presidential palace in Kiev, was welcomed by Ukraine President Volodymyr Zelensky. An honor guard was present and the two countries’ national anthems were played. Prime Minister Netanyahu and President Zelensky are currently holding a private meeting. They will then hold an expanded meeting with the two sides’ entourages. Minister Zeev Elkin and Deputy Foreign Minister Tzipi Hotovely will attend the expanded meeting.
