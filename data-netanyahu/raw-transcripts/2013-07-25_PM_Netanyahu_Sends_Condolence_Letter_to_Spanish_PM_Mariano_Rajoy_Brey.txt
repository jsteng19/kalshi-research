Following is the text of the letter that Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu sent to Spanish Prime Minister Mariano Rajoy Brey, today (Thursday, 25 July 2013), regarding the train accident in north-western Spain:_____

Dear Mr. Prime Minister,

On behalf of the Government and people of Israel, I extend my heartfelt condolences for the many human lives lost in yesterday's horrific train accident near Santiago de Compostela.

As always, Israel stands ready to assist the people of Spain in any way it can.

In friendship,

Benjamin Netanyahu_____
