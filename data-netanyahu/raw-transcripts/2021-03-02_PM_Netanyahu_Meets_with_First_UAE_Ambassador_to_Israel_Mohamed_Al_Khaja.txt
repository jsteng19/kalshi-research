Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today , at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with the first United Arab Emirates Ambassador to Israel, Mohammed Al Khaja.

Prime Minister Netanyahu told the ambassador that he was happy to see him and congratulated him on his arrival in Israel. "We're changing the Middle East. We're changing the world," the Prime Minister said.

The two discussed the potential in developing joint regional and bilateral projects in a broad range of fields.

National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat and additional senior officials also attended the meeting, which was warm and friendly.
