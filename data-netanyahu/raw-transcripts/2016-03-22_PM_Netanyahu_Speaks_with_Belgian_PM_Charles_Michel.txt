Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke by telephone with Belgian Prime Minister Charles Michel and, on behalf of the people of Israel, expressed condolences as well as wishes for a quick recovery to those who were injured in the attacks. The Prime Minister told his Belgian counterpart that terrorism does not distinguish between countries and offered Israel's help and cooperation in the fight against terrorism. The two leaders agreed to meet soon.
