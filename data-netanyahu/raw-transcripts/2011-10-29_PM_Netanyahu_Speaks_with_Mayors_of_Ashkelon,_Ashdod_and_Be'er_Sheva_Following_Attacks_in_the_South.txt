Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu tonight, spoke with Ashkelon Mayor Benny Vaknin, Ashdod Mayor Yehiel Lasri and Be'er Sheva Mayor Rubik Danilovitz.

Prime Minister Netanyahu asked to convey to the residents of the south his appreciation of their fortitude and stamina. He promised that the harsh response by the IDF, which struck at three launching cells, will be harsher still if need be.
