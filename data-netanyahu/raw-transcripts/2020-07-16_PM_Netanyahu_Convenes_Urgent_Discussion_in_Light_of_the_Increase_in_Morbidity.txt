Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/event_discussion160720

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister’s Office in Jerusalem, convened an urgent discussion – in light of the increase in morbidity – with the Defense Minister, the Health Minister, the Finance Minister, the Foreign Minister, the Science and Technology Minister, the Interior Minister, the Construction and Housing Minister, the head of the National Security Council and the Health Ministry Director General. Prime Minister Netanyahu at the start of the discussion: “Following the jump in morbidity to around 1,800 cases and the sharp increase in the doubling of the number of severe cases every seven days, I have just spoken with Alternate Prime Minister and Defense Minister Benny Gantz, National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat and Science minister Yizhar Shai about the possibility of interim steps that would prevent the need for a general lockdown. This is the goal of this discussion, to take interim steps to prevent a general lockdown in light of the gigantic jump in morbidity to around 1,800 cases and the doubling of the number of severe cases every seven days. This is the goal.” The Cabinet will convene this evening to approve the recommendations that were unanimously approved at the discussion.
