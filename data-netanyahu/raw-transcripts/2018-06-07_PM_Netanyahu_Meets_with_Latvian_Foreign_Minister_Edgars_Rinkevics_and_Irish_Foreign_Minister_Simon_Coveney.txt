Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Latvian Foreign Minister Edgars Rinkevics. The two discussed Iranian aggression, the latest developments in the region and the strengthening of bilateral relations. The Latvian Foreign Minister invited the Prime Minister to visit Latvia.

Prime Minister Netanyahu is currently meeting with Irish Foreign Minister Simon Coveney.
