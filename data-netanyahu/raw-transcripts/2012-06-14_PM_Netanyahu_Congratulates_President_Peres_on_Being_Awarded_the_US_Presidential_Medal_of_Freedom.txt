Prime Minister Netanyahu, early this morning, spoke with President Shimon Peres and congratulated him on being awarded the US Presidential Medal of Freedom.

Prime Minister Netanyahu told President Peres, "Shimon, congratulations. You join a small and distinguished list. You are worthy of the Medal and it is worthy of you. I think that this is a great honor for the State of Israel as well." The President replied, "Thank you for your dear and heartfelt words. Thank you from my heart."
