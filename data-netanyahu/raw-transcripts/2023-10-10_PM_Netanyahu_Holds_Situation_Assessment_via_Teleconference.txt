Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, held a situation assessment via teleconference.

Participating in the call were the Defense Minister, the Strategic Affairs Minister, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the Director of the National Security Council, the Director of the ISA, the Director of the Mossad, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Cabinet Secretary, the Head of IDF Intelligence, the Head of IDF Operations, the Head of the IDF Strategic Branch, the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories and the Head of Research at IDF Intelligence.
