Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu monitored the operation of the second phase of the release of our hostages from the IDF Operations Branch control center at the Kirya in Tel Aviv.

The Prime Minister received regular updates from the Head of the Operations Dept. at the IDF Operations Branch, Col. Nimrod Chibolsky and also spoke with the commander of the intelligence effort on the return of the hostages and missing, Maj.-Gen. Nitzan Alon regarding the operation.
