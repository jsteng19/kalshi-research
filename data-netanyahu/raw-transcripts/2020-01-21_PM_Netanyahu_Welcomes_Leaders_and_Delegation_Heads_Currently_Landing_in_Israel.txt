Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, welcomed the leaders and delegation heads who are currently landing in Israel: "I welcome the leaders from around the world who are coming here, to Jerusalem, to mark – together with us – 75 years since the liberation of the Auschwitz extermination camp. It is important that they remember where we came from, and it is important that they see where we have gone."
