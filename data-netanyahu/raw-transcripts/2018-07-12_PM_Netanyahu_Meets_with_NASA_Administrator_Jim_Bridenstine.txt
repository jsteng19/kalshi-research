Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with NASA Administrator Jim Bridenstine. Science and Technology Minister Ofir Akunis also attended the meeting.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked NASA Administrator Bridenstine for choosing Israel as a destination on his first visit [abroad] since assuming office on 23 April of this year. The Prime Minister also expressed interest in deepening relations between NASA and Israel.
