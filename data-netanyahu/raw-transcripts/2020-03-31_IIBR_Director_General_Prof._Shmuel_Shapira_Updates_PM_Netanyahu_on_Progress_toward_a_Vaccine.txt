Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, spoke with Israel Institute for Biological Research Director General Prof. Shmuel Shapira, who updated him on progress in the institute's research and development efforts regarding a vaccine and antibodies.

Prof. Shapira noted that there has been significant progress in planning for the vaccine and added that preparations are now being made ahead of a model for the start of experimentation on animals.
