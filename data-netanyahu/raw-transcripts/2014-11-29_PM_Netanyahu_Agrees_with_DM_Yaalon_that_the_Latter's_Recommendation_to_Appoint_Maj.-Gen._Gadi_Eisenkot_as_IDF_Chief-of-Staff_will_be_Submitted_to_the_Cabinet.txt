In continuation of his meetings in recent weeks with candidates for the post of IDF Chief-of-Staff and pursuant to his meeting yesterday morning, with Maj.-Gen. Gadi Eisenkot, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has agreed with Defense Minister Moshe Yaalon that the latter's recommendation to appoint Maj.-Gen. Eisenkot as the 21st IDF Chief-of-Staff will be submitted to the Cabinet.

"Maj.-Gen. Eisenkot has been chosen from an excellent group of major generals to lead the IDF in the face of the complex security challenges facing the State of Israel. On behalf of the citizens of Israel, I wish him success," the Prime Minister said.
