Prime Minister Netanyahu and Health Minister Yuli Edelstein will, at Sheba Medical Center at Tel Hashomer, be vaccinated against the coronavirus. The Prime Minister will thus be among the first world leaders to be vaccinated against the coronavirus.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and Health Minister Edelstein, who will open the vaccine operation in Israel, will be vaccinated on in front of the cameras on a live broadcast in order to serve as personal examples and encourage the public at large to be vaccinated.

Prime Minister Netanyahu will be accompanied by his personal physician, Dr. Tzvi Herman Berkowitz.

Coverage details will be issued separately.
