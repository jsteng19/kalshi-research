Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu in his Jerusalem office, met with Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople Bartholomew I and other senior Eastern Orthodox church officials from the US and Europe. The Prime Minister said, "Our relations are good and will continue to be good, but it's not merely our relations. It's the whole approach we have to all faiths and of course all denominations and all churches and all movements. We're an open society with religious freedom."
