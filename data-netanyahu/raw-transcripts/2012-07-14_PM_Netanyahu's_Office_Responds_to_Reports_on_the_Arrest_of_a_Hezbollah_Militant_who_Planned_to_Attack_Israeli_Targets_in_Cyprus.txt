Regarding

reports on the arrest of a Hezbollah militant who planned to attack Israeli targets in Cyprus

, the Prime Minister's Office releases the following:

"There are no borders to Iranian terrorism. After Iran sent its people to assassinate – on US soil – the Saudi Arabian ambassador and to perpetrate terrorist attacks in Azerbaijan, Bangkok, Tbilisi, New Delhi and Africa, its intention to perpetrate attacks in Cyprus has now been exposed. The international community needs to fight the greatest exporter of terrorism in the world."
