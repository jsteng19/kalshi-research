Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister’s Office in Jerusalem, met with new Falash Mura olim and their families. Pursuant to the Government decision – formulated by Prime Minister’s Office Director General Ronen Peretz – to bring another approximately 400 olim from Ethiopia, the Prime Minister, the Aliyah and Absorption Minister and the Communications Minister met with several Falash Mura from the first group of new olim, which arrived in Israel yesterday. The olim were reunited in Israel with family members who accompanied them to the meeting. MK Gadi Barkan, former MK Avraham Neguise and Aliyah and Absorption Ministry Director of Ethiopian Absorption David Yasu also attended the meeting.
