Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara Veterans Day in the US, visited Mount Vernon, the home of the first President of the United States, George Washington. They paid their respects to the first US president, met with a veteran and attended a memorial ceremony.

Prime Minister Netanyahu: "It is very moving to stand in this place and pay respects to the first president of the US, who was a great general and the father of the American nation."
