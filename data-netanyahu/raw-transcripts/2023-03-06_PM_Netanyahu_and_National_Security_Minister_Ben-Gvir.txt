Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu to Border Police Officers: May G-d grant you success and that you watch over the country, yourselves and each other."

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and National Security Minister Itamar Ben-Gvir this evening, at the synagogue at the Beit Horon Border Police base, attended a Scroll of Esther reading for Purim, together with Knesset Speaker Amir Ohana, Israel Police Inspector General Kobi Shabtai, the Border Police Commander and Border Police officers.

Prime Minister Netanyahu after the reading:

"I, along with the people of Israel, greatly appreciate your actions, your efforts, your heroism and your sacrifice.

May G-d grant you success and that you watch over the country, yourselves and each other.

Happy Purim to you."
