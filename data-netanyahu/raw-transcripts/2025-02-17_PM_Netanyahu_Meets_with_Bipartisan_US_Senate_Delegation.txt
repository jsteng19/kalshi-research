Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Kirya in Tel Aviv, met with a bipartisan US Senate delegation led by Lindsey Graham (R-SC). Also participating in the meeting were Senators Dan Sullivan (R-AK), Sheldon Whitehouse (D-RI), Richard Blumenthal (D-CT), Joni Ernst (R-IA), Adam Schiff (D-CA) and Andy Kim (D-NJ).
