Due to the security challenges facing the State of Israel, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has asked Mossad Director Yossi Cohen to extend his tenure by an additional six months. In June 2021, the Director of the Mossad will be replaced. Mossad Director Cohen acceded to Prime Minister Netanyahu's request; in January 2021, he will begin his sixth year in office.
