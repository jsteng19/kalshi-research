Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening (Monday, 10 November 2014), at his Jerusalem office, convened a security discussion. Participating in the discussion were Defense Minister Moshe Yaalon, Public Defense Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch, IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Benny Gantz, ISA Director Yoram Cohen, Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Yohanan Danino and Attorney General Yehuda Weinstein. The Prime Minister ordered that a series of steps be taken against terrorists including increasing the number of security forces on the ground and moving forward on the demolition of terrorists' homes.
