Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu expressed his sorrow on the passing of Maj.-Gen. (ret.) Avraham "Bren" Adan. "A courageous fighter and commander, who symbolized the State of Israel, has passed away. His legacy, which is synonymous with the history of the State of Israel, will remain with us forever," the Prime Minister said.
