Palestinian Authority Chairman Abu Mazen this afternoon called Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and wished him a Happy Passover.

The Prime Minister recalled the cooperation between them – and the talks that they held – in the past, and noted that he intended to do so again in the future in order to advance peace between Israel and the Palestinians.

PA Chairman Abu Mazen greeted Prime Minister Netanyahu and his family and said that both sides needed to work towards peace.

Prime Minister Netanyahu's Bureau notes that the conversation between the two men was warm and friendly.
