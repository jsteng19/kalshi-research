Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/event-caribbean210923

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, yesterday, on the sidelines of the UN General Assembly in New York City, met with leaders of Caribbean island states, including the President of the Dominican Republic, Luis Abinader, the Prime Minister of Antigua and Barbuda, Gaston Browne, Haitian Prime Minister Ariel Henry, St. Kitts and Nevis Prime Minister Terrance Drew and the Foreign Minister of Barbados, Kerrie Symmonds The leaders presented the current challenges their countries face in managing water resources and public health. Prime Minister Netanyahu directed that a delegation of Israeli experts go to the Caribbean and evaluate the needs and propose assistance programs in these areas.
