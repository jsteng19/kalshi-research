Pursuant to his 3 February 2020 meeting with Sovereignty Council of Sudan Chairman Abdel Fattah al Burhan, Prime Minister Netanyahu welcomes the position of the Sudanese Foreign Ministry, which reflects the brave decision of the Sovereignty Council Chairman to work toward advancing relations between the two countries.

Prime Minister Netanyahu:

"Israel, Sudan and the entire region will benefit from the peace agreement and will be able – together – to build a better future for all peoples of the region. We will do whatever is necessary to turn vision into reality."
