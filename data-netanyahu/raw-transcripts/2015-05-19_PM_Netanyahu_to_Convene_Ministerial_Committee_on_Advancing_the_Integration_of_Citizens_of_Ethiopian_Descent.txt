Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu will, next week, convene a meeting of the ministerial committee on advancing the integration of Israeli citizens of Ethiopian descent into Israeli society, which he will chair.

At the start of today's Cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Netanyahu said:

"Today I asked to form a ministerial committee under my leadership to deal with the plight of Israeli citizens of Ethiopian descent. They deserve equal treatment, they have rights and we will see to it that they enjoy these rights like all Israeli citizens."
