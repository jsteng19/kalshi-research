Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's special envoy, attorney Yitzhak Molcho, met this evening, for over two hours, with representatives of the international Quartet in an effort to find a way to resume direct negotiations between Israel and the Palestinians. This is in keeping with the Quartet's outline, according to which the resumption of direct negotiations is to be done without preconditions. It was decided that the sides would coordinate on a date to resume their discussions.
