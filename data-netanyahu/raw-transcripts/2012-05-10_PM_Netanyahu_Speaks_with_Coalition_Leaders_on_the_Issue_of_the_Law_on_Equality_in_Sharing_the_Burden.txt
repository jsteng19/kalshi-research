Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, spoke with the coalition leaders. It was agreed that discussions on the issue of submitting an alternative to the Tal Law will begin next week in order to enable the new law to be approved within two-and-a-half months.

The new law will gradually increase equality in sharing the burden and will introduce Jews and Arabs into the service cycle without setting public against public.
