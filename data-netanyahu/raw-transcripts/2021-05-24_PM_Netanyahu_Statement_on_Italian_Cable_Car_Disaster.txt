Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke_condolence240521

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening :

"We all send condolences from the depths of our hearts to the Biran family following the terrible disaster in Italy in which Amit and Tal Peleg-Biran, their two-year-old son Tom Biran, and Tal's grandparents, Barbara and Yitzhak Cohen perished; may their memories be blessed. We are praying for the health of Eitan, Amit and Tal's other son, who is in hospital. The State of Israel will assist the family with anything it needs following this terrible tragedy."
