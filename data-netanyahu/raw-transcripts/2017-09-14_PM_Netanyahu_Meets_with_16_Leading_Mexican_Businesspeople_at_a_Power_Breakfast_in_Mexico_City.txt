Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, in Mexico City, at a power breakfast, met with 16 leading Mexican businesspeople and discussed with them opportunities in the Israeli market and the potential for investment in advanced Israeli technologies.

Prime Minister Netanyahu briefed the businesspeople on the developing Israeli cyber and vehicle industries and called on them to invest in Israel. He also invited them to hold their next meeting in Israel.
