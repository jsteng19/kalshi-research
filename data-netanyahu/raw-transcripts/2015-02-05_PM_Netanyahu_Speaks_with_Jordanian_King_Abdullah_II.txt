Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke this afternoon with King Abdullah II of Jordan. Prime Minister Netanyahu extended his condolences to the King and to the Jordanian people following the despicable murder of the Jordanian Air Force pilot.

Prime Minister Netanyahu added that all civilized people are shocked by this barbaric cruelty, which the world must fight. The Prime Minister noted the importance of the Jordanian ambassador's return to Israel and of the joint commitment to maintaining the status quo at the holy sites.
