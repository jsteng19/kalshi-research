Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-election271223

The Government, today, unanimously approved the compromise proposed by Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu regarding the date for local elections.

The Government has directed the IDF to submit to the Government Secretariat, by 14:00 tomorrow (Thursday, 28 December 2023), its position on the possibility of discharging 2,189 candidates in the local elections who are currently on active reserve duty, who among them cannot be discharged, and a breakdown of the local authorities in which there are candidates who cannot be discharged.

On Sunday (31 December 2023), the Government will hold an additional discussion, when it will have the complete data from the IDF and when it will decide on the date of the elections.
