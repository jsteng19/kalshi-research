Prime Minister Netanyahu, held an economic consultation with the economic team. Finance Minister Moshe Kahlon, Economy and Industry Minister Eli Cohen, National Security head Meir Cohen, Bank of Israel Governor Prof. Amir Yaron, National Economic Council head Prof. Avi Simhon, Prime Minister's Office Director General Ronen Peretz, Finance Ministry Director General Shai Babad and other officials also participated.

Prime Minister Netanyahu instructed the economic team to present – in the coming days – a strategic plan for managing the economy as well as an updated, extensive and significant relief package including assistance for the self-employed, wage earners, businesses and households.

Several of the plans within the assistance package will be submitted for Knesset approval.
