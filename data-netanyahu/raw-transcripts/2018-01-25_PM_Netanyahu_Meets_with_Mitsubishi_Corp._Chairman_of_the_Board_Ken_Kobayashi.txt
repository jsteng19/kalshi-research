Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, in Davos, met with the Chairman of the Board of Mitsubishi Corporation, Ken Kobayashi. The Prime Minister briefed him on potential opportunities in the Israeli market and told him that investment in Israel is worthwhile.

Mitsubishi Chairman Kobayashi said that as a result of Prime Minister Netanyahu's meeting with Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe, Israel is a major player on Mitsubishi's map and added that they are now starting to consider investments in cyber and other areas. He noted that he intends to send a delegation to Israel soon in order to evaluate possibilities for investment and cooperation.

In recent months Mitsubishi representatives have begun to consider expanding company operations in Israel with emphasis on technological cooperation including investments in Israeli companies.
