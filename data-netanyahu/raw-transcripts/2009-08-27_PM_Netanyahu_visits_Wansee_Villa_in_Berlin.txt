Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, during his visit to Germany , visited the Wansee villa. The Prime Minister said, "My friend, Minister Yossi Peled, who lost most of his family in the Holocaust, and my wife, whose father was almost the only surviving member, and fellow members of the entourage: Here in this place, the final solution of the Jewish People was planned in detail. And here in this place, as Prime Minister of the State of Israel, I have signed three Hebrew words in the guestbook: Am Yisrael Chai, The People of Israel Live."
