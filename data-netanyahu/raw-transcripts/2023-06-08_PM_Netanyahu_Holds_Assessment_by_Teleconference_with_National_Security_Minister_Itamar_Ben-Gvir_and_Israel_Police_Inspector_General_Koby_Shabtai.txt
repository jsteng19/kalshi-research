Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, held an assessment by teleconference with National Security Minister Itamar Ben-Gvir and Israel Police Inspector General Koby Shabtai following the murder near Nazareth.

Prime Minister Netanyahu directed that steps be taken immediately to find the murderers and that determined action be taken to eradicate the plague of violence and murder in the Arab sector.
