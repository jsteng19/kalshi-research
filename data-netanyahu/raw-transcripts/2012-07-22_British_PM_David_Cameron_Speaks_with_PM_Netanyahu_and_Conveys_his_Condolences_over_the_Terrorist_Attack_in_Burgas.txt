British Prime Minister David Cameron, this morning spoke with Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and expressed his condolences over

the terrorist attack in Burgas

. He also reiterated Britain's commitment to fight global terrorism.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked his British counterpart for his remarks and made it clear that a price must be exacted from terrorists and those who dispatch them He also wished Britain success in hosting the Olympic Games, which are due to open over the weekend.
