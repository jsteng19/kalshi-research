Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu met with Nauru President Baron Waqa.

Nauru has friendly relations with the State of Israel. President Waqa asked Prime Minister Netanyahu that Israel assist his country in medical training, water, waste management and innovation.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "You have been our wonderful friend. It's a pleasure to receive you in the land of Israel and the State of Israel. And I hope you have an excellent visit that reflects our excellent relations."
