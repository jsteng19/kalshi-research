Prime Minister Netanyahu met today with Ambassador Susan Rice. The hour long meeting was very friendly and a wide range of issues was discussed.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked Ambassador Rice for the Obama administration’s strong opposition to the Goldstone report and for her consistent support at the United Nations. Ambassador Rice reiterated the Obama administration’s commitment to Israel’s security and its determination to advance peace in the region.
