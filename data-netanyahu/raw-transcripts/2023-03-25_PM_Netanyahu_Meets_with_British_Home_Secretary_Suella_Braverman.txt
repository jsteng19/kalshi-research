Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, last night, in the context of his diplomatic visit to London, met with British Home Secretary Suella Braverman. They discussed the close working ties between Israel and Britain on domestic security, the campaign against the Iranian nuclear program and counter-terrorism. They agreed to strengthen bilateral cooperation and expand it to additional areas, including cyber-defense and aviation in the framework of the war on crime and money-laundering. Also attending the meeting were National Security Council Director Tzachi Hanegbi, Prime Minister's Military Secretary Brig.-Gen. Avi Gil and Israeli Ambassador to Britain Tzipi Hotovely.
