Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today in his Jerusalem office, met with orphaned boys who were celebrating their bar mitzvah. The boys told the Prime Minister that one of them, Yonatan Amzaleg, was named after his brother, Yonatan Netanyahu, and that he himself had attended his brit milah 13 years ago. Prime Minister Netanyahu congratulated the boys and said that, "A bar mitzvah is a very moving event. I hope that this day will remain etched in your memories. We always say that there is no joy in life without sorrow and I know that each one of you has experienced the tragedy of loss. Remember that the Jewish People supports and embraces you."
