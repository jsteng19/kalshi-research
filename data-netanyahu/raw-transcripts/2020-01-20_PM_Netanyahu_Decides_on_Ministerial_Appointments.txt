Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, decided on the following ministerial appointments: * Minister Tzachi Hanegbi will be appointed Agriculture and Rural Development Minister, in addition to his post as Regional Cooperation Minister. * Minister Ofir Akunis will be appointed Labor, Social Affairs and Social Services Minister, in addition to his post as Science and Technology Minister. * Deputy Minister Tzipi Hotovely will be appointed Diaspora Affairs Minister. (No deputy minister will be appointed in her place at the Foreign Ministry.) * Deputy Minister Meshulam Nahari will be appointed Deputy Labor, Social Affairs and Social Services Minister instead of his post as Deputy Minister in the Interior and Periphery Development ministries.
