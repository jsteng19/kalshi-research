Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke by telephone with Egyptian President Abdel Fattah El-Sisi. The Prime Minister thanked President El-Sisi for Egypt's support in advancing stability in the region, especially in Gaza, and asked that he continue assisting in the return of our prisoners and our missing. The leaders discussed regional challenges and the strengthening of relations and cooperation in a range of areas. They welcomed the development of relations with the United Arab Emirates.
