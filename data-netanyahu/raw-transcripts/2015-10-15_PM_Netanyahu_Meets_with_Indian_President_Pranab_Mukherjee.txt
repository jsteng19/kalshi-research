Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, in Jerusalem, met with Indian President Pranab Mukherjee. The two discussed bilateral cooperation in the fields of security, technology, innovation and agriculture. They also discussed counterterrorism and advanced policing methods, and agreed to enhance bilateral cooperation in this area, including ministerial visits.

Indian President Mukherjee invited Prime Minister Netanyahu to make an official visit to India. The Prime Minister thanked him for the invitation and that he would be pleased to accept.
