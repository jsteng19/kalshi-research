Russian President Vladimir Putin, a short while ago today telephoned Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and expressed his condolences over the passing of former President Shimon Peres. He noted his high esteem for the late former president as an important leader of the State of Israel.

Russian President Putin told Prime Minister Netanyahu that he ascribes great importance to the continuation of the friendship between Russia and Israel.
