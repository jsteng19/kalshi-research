Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu commented as follows on the Standard & Poor's statement regarding Israel's credit rating

"This is the result of the responsible economic policy that we have been taking in recent years. We must make continue making it clear to international markets that we intend to continue this responsible policy so that Israel will maintain its high credit rating. This has great influence on the stability and prosperity of our economy."
