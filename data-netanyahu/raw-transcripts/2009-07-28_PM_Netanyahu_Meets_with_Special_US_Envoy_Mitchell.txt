Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this morning held a 2.5-hour working meeting with special US envoy George Mitchell. The meeting was friendly and positive and encompassed the entire range of issues on the agenda.

At the end of the meeting, Prime Minister Netanyahu said that Israel is doing its utmost to advance the peace process with the Palestinians. Envoy Mitchell expressed the hope that the sides are close to reaching understandings that will constitute a basis for the continuation of the diplomatic process.
