Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today sent a condolence letter to Brazil President Dilma Vana Rousseff regarding the fire in Santa Maria that claimed so many victims.

Prime Minister Netanyahu wrote: "On behalf of the Government and people of Israel, I express my deepest condolences regarding the tragic fire that occurred in the nightclub in Santa Maria that claimed over 230 innocent victims. We wish a quick recovery to those who were injured and are ready to provide you and your people with whatever assistance that may be required. At this difficult time, our thoughts and prayers are with the families of the victims and the Brazilian people."
