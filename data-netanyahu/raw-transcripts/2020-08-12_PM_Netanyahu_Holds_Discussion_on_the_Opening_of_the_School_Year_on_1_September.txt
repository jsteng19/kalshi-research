Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke_discussion120820

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister’s Office in Jerusalem, held a discussion with Education Minister Yoav Galant, Health Minister Yuli Edelstein, Finance Minister Yisrael Katz and National Security Council (NSC) head Meir Ben-Shabbat in order to ensure the opening of the school year as planned on 1 September 2020.

Prime Minister Netanyahu:

“I have just held a meeting with the ministers of Education, Health and Finance, and the head of the NSC, on the opening of the school year. We are doing everything so that the school year opens on 1 September. That is the target date; it will not change. We are also concerned that there will be all of the means, and financial, material and organizational resources to do this, which is important to all citizens of Israel.”
