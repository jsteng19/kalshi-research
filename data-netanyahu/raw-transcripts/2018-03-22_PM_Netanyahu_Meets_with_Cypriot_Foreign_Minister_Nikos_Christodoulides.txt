Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Cypriot Foreign Minister Nikos Christodoulides. The two discussed regional developments and the strengthening of bilateral ties in energy and emergency situations.

The meeting was held ahead of the fourth trilateral meeting between the leaders of Israel, Greece and Cyprus, which is due to be held in Cyprus in the coming months. Prime Minister Netanyahu congratulated Foreign Minister Christodoulides on taking office.
