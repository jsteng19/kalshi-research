Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today : "I send my deepest condolences to the family of the late Cpl. Dennis Zinobayev from the Kfir Brigade, who was killed last night in an explosion of munitions. May his memory be for a blessing. I send my wishes for a swift recovery to the soldiers who were wounded in the incident."
