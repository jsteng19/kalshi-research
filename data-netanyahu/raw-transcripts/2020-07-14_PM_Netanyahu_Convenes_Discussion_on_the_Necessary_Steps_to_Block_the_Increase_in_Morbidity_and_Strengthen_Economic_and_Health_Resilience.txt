Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, convened a discussion on the necessary steps to block the increase in morbidity and to strengthen the economic and health resilience. The formulation of a long-term strategy was also discussed. Defense Minister and Alternate Prime Minister Benny Gantz, Finance Minister Yisrael Katz, Health Minister Yuli Edelstein, Science and Technology Minister Yizhar Shai, National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat, head of the Prime Minister's Staff Asher Hayon, National Economic Council head Prof. Avi Simhon, Bank of Israel Governor Prof. Amir Yaron, Prime Minister's Office Acting Director General Ronen Peretz, Health Ministry Director General Prof. Chezy Levy, Finance Ministry Budget Director Shaul Meridor, Moshe Bar Siman Tov, Prof. Uri Hefetz, Prof. Naomi Feldman, Dr. Limor Samimian-Darash, Kobi Haver and Prof. Ran Balicer. Prime Minister Netanyahu directed that the health system be strengthened with additional positions, budgets and personnel. The Prime Minister said that additional financial assistance for families had to be formulated. The forum will meet again in the coming days.
