Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu called US Vice President Joe Biden and expressed condolences on behalf of himself, his wife Sara and the people of Israel on the passing of Beau Biden this past Saturday.

Prime Minister Netanyahu told US Vice President Biden that he recalled the pride with which the latter – during their 20 years of friendship – would tell him about Beau and about the deep bond between them.

The Prime Minister said that he offered his support, and that of the people of Israel, at this difficult time, and sent his condolences.
