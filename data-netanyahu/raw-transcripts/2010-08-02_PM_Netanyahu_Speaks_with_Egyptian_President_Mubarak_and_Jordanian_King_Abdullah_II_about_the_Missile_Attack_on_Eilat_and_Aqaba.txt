Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening (Monday), 2.8.10, spoke by telephone with Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak and Jordanian King Abdullah II about the missile attack on Eilat and Aqaba. The Prime Minister said that, "This deplorable attack on innocent civilians in Israel and Jordan was perpetrated by terrorists who want to sabotage the peace process. All countries in the region that aspire towards peace need to fight these forces in order to repel terrorism and bring peace closer."
