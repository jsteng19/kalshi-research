Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this morning in his Jerusalem office, met with Kenyan Prime Minister Raila Odinga. The meeting was in continuation of the deepening of Israel's relations with African countries and follows the Prime Minister's

meeting yesterday with Ugandan President Yoweri Museveini

and

his 22.9.11 meeting with South Sudan President Salva Kiir Mayardit

at the UN General Assembly.
