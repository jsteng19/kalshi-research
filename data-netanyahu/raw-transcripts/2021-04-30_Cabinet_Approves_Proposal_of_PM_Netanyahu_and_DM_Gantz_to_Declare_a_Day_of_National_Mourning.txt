The Cabinet, today, in a conference call, approved the proposal of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Defense Minister Benny Gantz that, in wake of the Mt. Meron disaster, a day of national mourning will be declared on Sunday (2 May 2021). National flags will be lowered to half-mast at public buildings, IDF bases and Israeli missions abroad.
