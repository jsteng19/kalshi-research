PM Netanyahu, in Washington, met with Guatemalan President Jimmy Morales and said at the start of the meeting:

"Thank you for everything you're doing, for recognizing Jerusalem as Israel's capital. I hope soon you will move your embassy to Jerusalem; we hope."

Guatemalan President Morales replied:

"It is an honor and it is the right thing to do."

The two discussed deepening bilateral ties and cooperation. Guatemalan President Morales invited Prime Minister Netanyahu to visit his country. The Prime Minister thanked him for the invitation and also invited him to visit Israel.
