Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu expresses condolences and sorrow over the passing of former Labor Party Chairman and Defense Minister Binyamin ("Fouad") Ben-Eliezer.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "Fouad served the State of Israel for decades as a fighter, commander, public servant and senior government minister. I knew him and I esteemed his contribution and his special personality. In my many conversations with him, Fouad expressed his concern for – and commitment to – the future of the State of Israel that he loved so much. May his memory be blessed."
