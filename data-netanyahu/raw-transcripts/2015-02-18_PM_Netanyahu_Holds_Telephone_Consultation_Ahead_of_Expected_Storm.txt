Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, ahead of the expected storm, held a telephone consultation with the participation of Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch, Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Yohanan Danino, GOC Home Front Command Maj.-Gen. Eyal Eisenberg and Jerusalem Mayor Nir Barkat.

Public Security Minister Aharonovitch noted that emergency and rescue services are prepared for the storm and are working in coordination with local authorities. He emphasized that residents must listen to instructions and directives from the Israel Police and other authorized agencies.

At the end of the discussion, Prime Minister Netanyahu said: "The supreme goal for everyone is to save lives and facilitate essential services for residents in storm-affected areas."
