Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu held discussions on the situation regarding the production, acquisition and procurement of vaccines and on the increasing the use of rapid coronavirus testing kits. Attending the discussion on vaccines were Health Minister Yuli Edelstein, the head of the National Security Council, the Health Ministry Director General, the national coronavirus project manager, the head of the Israel Institute for Biological Research (IIBR), the Prime Minister's adviser on coronavirus affairs, the Defense Minister's assistant on self-defense, the director of the Health Ministry Medical Technology unit and other officials. Participants in the discussion were briefed on the efforts in various fields. Alongside the development efforts at the IIBR, Prime Minister Netanyahu directed that action be taken to create redundancies and varied responses, without budgetary impediments. Participating in the discussion on technologies and rapid testing kits were Health Minister Edelstein, Science Minister Shai, Deputy Health Minister Kisch, the head of the National Security Council, the Health Ministry Director General, the national coronavirus project manager and senior Health Ministry officials.
