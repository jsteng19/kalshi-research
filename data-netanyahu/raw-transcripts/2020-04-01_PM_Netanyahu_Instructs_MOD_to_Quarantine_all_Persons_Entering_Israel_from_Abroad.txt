Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, instructed the Ministry of Defense (MOD) to quarantine all persons entering Israel from abroad in designated hotels (or other installations converted for that purpose) under supervision. The decision is to be implemented immediately.

Prime Minister Netanyahu also determined that to the extent that MOD succeeds in supplying test kits, in accordance with the requirements set by the Ministry of Health, the carrying out of the tests will be considered in relation to all those returning from abroad.
