Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, in New York City, met with Rwandan President Paul Kagame. The two leaders discussed regional threats and the Iranian aggression. They also discussed the situation in the Horn of Africa with emphasis on the peace agreement between Ethiopia and Eritrea and the concomitant opportunities for establishing stability in the region..

Prime Minister Netanyahu asked for Rwandan President Kagame's assistance in advancing ties with additional African countries.
