Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening spoke with Tali Yehoshua-Koren, who was wounded in

the New Delhi terrorist attack

and has recently returned to Israel, and told her, "We have monitored your situation with both worry and admiration. You made a courageous and resourceful effort. All of Israel congratulates you on coming out of it alive and we are pleased that you are back here with us."

Tali, who is hospitalized at the

Tel Aviv Sourasky Medical Center

thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu for phoning and told him, "I feel better and would like to thank everyone for their support."
