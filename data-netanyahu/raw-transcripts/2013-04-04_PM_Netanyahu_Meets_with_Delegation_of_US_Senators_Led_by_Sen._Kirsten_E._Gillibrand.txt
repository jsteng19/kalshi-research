Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening met with a group of US Senators led by Sen. Kirsten E. Gillibrand (D-NY). At the start of the meeting, the Prime Minister said, "Today it is more clear than ever before that we simply cannot allow a situation in which a regime that calls for our annihilation obtains the weapons of annihilation. This must be prevented at all costs."
