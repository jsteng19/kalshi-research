Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, Tourism Minister Stas Misezhnikov, Environmental Protection Minister Gilad Erdan, Finance Minister Yuval Steinitz, Interior Minister Eli Yishai and Justice Minister Yaakov Neeman today during a tour of the Dead Sea area, voted for the Dead Sea in the Finals of the New 7 Wonders of Nature Competition. The Prime Minister praised the Dead Sea and said that, "Whoever has been by it or in it knows how unique it is. It is one of the seven wonders of the world and we have one message: Go and vote." The Dead Sea is competing against 27 other global sites in the competition. The seven winners are due to be announced in November 2011. One may vote for the Dead Sea via the above link or by sending an SMS to 2244 with the words "Dead Sea" in Hebrew, English or Arabic.
