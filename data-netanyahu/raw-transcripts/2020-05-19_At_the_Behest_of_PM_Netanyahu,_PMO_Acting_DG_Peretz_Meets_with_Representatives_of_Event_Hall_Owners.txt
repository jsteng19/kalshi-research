At the behest of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, Prime Minister's Office Acting Director General Ronen Peretz, today , met with representatives of owners of event halls in order to discuss the outlines for assistance that are currently being evaluated for the sectors that have been hurt by the crisis.

Prime Minister's Office Acting Deputy Director General for Home Affairs Lior Farber, Hall and Garden Owners Association Chairman Noam Levy, Hall and Garden Owners Association Director General Aviram Alon and Hall and Garden Owners Association member and event hall owner Nir Shefer also participated.
