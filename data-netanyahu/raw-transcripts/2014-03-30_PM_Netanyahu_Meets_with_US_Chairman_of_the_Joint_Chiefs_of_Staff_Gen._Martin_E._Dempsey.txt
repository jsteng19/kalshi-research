Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, in his Jerusalem office, met with US Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Gen. Martin E. Dempsey. IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Benny Gantz also attended the meeting.

At the start of the meeting, Prime Minister Netanyahu welcomed Gen. Dempsey to Jerusalem again and said that his visit was an expression of the close ties between Israel and the US. The Prime Minister thanked Gen. Dempsey for the additional American assistance for anti-missile systems and noted the security cooperation between the two countries.
