Prime Minister, Benjamin Netanyahu, instructed Cabinet Secretary, Tzvi Hauser to take the necessary steps to organize the commemoration to the victims of the Carmel fire catastrophe.

For this purpose part of today's Ministerial Committee for Symbols and Ceremonies was dedicated to examining means and proposals to commemorate the victims. The proposals will be coordinated by the inter-ministerial advisory committee and will be prepared shortly.

The Government intends to hold commemorative events for the Carmel fire victims, 30 days after their tragic deaths.
