Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau, today, called Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu to congratulate him on forming a government. The two discussed the fight against the corona pandemic and agreed to cooperate on technology, with emphasis on finding drugs and a vaccine. Prime Minister Netanyahu conveyed his condolences and those of the citizens of Israel following the disasters that have struck Canada in recent days. The leaders also discussed the latest regional developments.
