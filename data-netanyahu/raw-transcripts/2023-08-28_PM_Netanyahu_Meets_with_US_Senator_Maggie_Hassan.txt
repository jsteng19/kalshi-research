Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with US Senator Maggie Hassan (D-NH). The sides discussed the major challenges and opportunities facing the US and Israel, and the need to cooperate in the field of artificial intelligence. Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked Senator Hassan for her consistent support of Israel. Also participating in the meeting were the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the Director of the National Security Council, the Prime Minister's Foreign Policy Adviser, and additional officials. Senator Hassan was accompanied by the Acting US Ambassador and the head of the US Embassy Political Department.
