Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu strongly condemns

the incident at an IDF base in Judea and Samaria today in which IDF vehicles were vandalized and graffiti was sprayed against IDF commanders

. He said that this was an abhorrent crime directed against commanders and vehicles the mission of which is to protect the lives of Israeli civilians in Judea and Samaria.

The Prime Minister ordered enforcement authorities to find the offenders and bring them to justice.
