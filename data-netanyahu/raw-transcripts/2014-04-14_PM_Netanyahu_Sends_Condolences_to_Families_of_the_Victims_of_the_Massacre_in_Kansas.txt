Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu sends his condolences to the families of the victims of yesterday's massacre in Overland Park, Kansas in the US. "We understand that the murders were -- according to indications -- were perpetrated out of hatred of Jews .The State of Israel is at the forefront, along with all civilized peoples which are committed to the struggle against this scourge," the Prime Minister said.
