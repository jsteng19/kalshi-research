Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, met with Ashkenazi Chief Rabbi David Lau, Sephardi Chief Rabbi Yitzhak Yosef, the Secretary of the Chief Rabbinate Council, the Chairman of the Vaad Hayeshivot and the leaders of the ultra-orthodox Knesset factions.

It was agreed that the guidelines of the Health Ministry must be upheld while finding solutions for Torah study
