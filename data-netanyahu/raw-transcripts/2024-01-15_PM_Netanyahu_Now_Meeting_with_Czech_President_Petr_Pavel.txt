Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today , at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met privately with Czech President Petr Pavel.

The two leaders are currently holding an expanded meeting. Participating for the Israeli side are National Security Council Director Tzachi Hanegbi, the Prime Minister's Foreign Policy Adviser, Dr. Ophir Falk, and Israeli Ambassador to the Czech Republic, Anna Azari.
