Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara at the Prime Minister's Residence in Jerusalem, hosted a dinner for Togo President Faure Gnassingbe, who wrote in the guestbook: "I dream of Israel's return to Africa and Africa's return to Israel."

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "Togo is a true friend of the State of Israel."
