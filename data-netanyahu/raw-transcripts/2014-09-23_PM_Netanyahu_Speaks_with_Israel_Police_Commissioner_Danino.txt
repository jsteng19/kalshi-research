Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today , spoke with Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Yohanan Danino and commended

the action of the Anti-Terrorist Unit

which, this morning, in a combined operation , hit the terrorists who murdered

Naftali Frankel, Gilad Sha'er and Eyal Yifrach

. The Prime Minister asked to convey his Rosh Hashanah greetings to the men and women of the Israel Police who work day and night throughout the country to protect Israel's citizens.
