Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, held a telephone consultation with Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch, Justice Minister Tzipi Livni, Israel Police Commissioner Insp.-Gen. Yohanan Danino and Attorney General Yehuda Weinstein. The Prime Minister was updated on the details of the incident and on the preliminary stages of the investigation.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "I regard this incident with utmost gravity. This is a threat to the rule of law. Top priority must be given to investigating this event and to treating those responsible to the fullest extent of the law. I trust that the police will bring the criminals to justice."
