Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu at the Presidential Palace in Vilnius, met with Lithuanian President Dalia Grybauskaite and made the following remarks at the start of their meeting:

"I have to tell you that this is not a regular visit for me. It's full of historical meaning, both the history of the Jews here in Lithuania and also my personal history, my family's personal history. My great-grandfather came from Lithuania."
