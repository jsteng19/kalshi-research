Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke_cairo050819

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu following the terrorist attack in Cairo: "Last night, terrorists detonated a car bomb near a hospital in central Cairo, killing 20 and wounding dozens of innocent Egyptians. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the families of the innocent victims, and wish a speedy recovery to the injured. We stand by the Egyptian people in their battle against terrorism."
