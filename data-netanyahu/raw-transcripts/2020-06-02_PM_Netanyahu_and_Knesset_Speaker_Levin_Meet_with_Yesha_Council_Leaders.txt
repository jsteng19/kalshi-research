Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, met with Knesset Speaker Yariv Levin and Yesha Council leaders. “We face a historic opportunity to apply sovereignty to areas of Judea and Samaria,” the Prime Minister said at the start of the meeting. Prime Minister Netanyahu reiterated his commitment to conduct negotiations based on the Trump plan. He said that the discussions with the Americans were still continuing. It was agreed that dialogue would continue. The Prime Minister and the Knesset Speaker called on the Yesha Council leaders to support this historic move.
