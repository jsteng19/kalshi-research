The Cabinet at its weekly meeting today approved Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's NIS 2 billion multi-year plan to develop and strengthen the Druze and Circassian communities. The plan touches on many areas of life including education, construction, infrastructures, social welfare and employment.

Prime Minister Netanyahu:

"The plan will help to reduce disparities and promote the population that serves in the IDF and sees itself as part of the State of Israel."
