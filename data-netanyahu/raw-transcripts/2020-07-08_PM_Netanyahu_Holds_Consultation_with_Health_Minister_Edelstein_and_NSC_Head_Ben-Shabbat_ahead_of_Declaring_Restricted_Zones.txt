Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has just held a consultation with Health Minister Yuli Edelstein and National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat, at which they discussed morbidity data at various centers ahead of declaring restricted zones. The Prime Minister directed that the Ministerial Committee on Declaring Restricted Zones be convened immediately following the Health Ministry's formulation of the final recommendations.
