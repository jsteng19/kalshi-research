Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, last night, called to congratulate Odelia Fitoussi, who was elected to the UN Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities. The Prime Minister congratulated her for this important achievement and said that he appreciates her ability and talents, which led to her election to a significant and important position in the international arena. He wished her success and said that all of Israel is proud of her.
