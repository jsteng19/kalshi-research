Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, met with 38 students at the National Security College who will, next week, finish their studies. The students are from the IDF, ISA, Prison Service, Israel Police, Foreign Ministry and Defense Ministry; there are also six overseas students from the USA, India, France, Singapore and Germany.

Prime Minister Netanyahu answered questions on diplomatic-security and socio-economic issues. He commented on US-Israel relations: "Israel-US relations are very durable and strong. This finds expression in the two countries' common policy because the Israeli and American peoples are similar in their character and values. In

my recent talks with US President Barack Obama

, I told him that the components of genuine peace are providing security for the State of Israel and Israelis, and dealing with

the Iranian nuclear issue
