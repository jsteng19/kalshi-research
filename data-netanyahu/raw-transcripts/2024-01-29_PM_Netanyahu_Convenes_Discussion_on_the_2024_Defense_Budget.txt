Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, at the Kirya in Tel Aviv, convened a meeting on the 2024 defense budget.

Participating in the discussion were the Defense Minister, the Finance Minister, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Chief-of-Staff, the Defense Ministry Director General, the Finance Ministry Director General, the Director of the National Economic Council, the Head of the National Security Council Defense Policy Division, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Head of the IDF Planning Directorate, the IDF Chief-of-Staff's Financial Adviser and the Head of the Finance Ministry Budget Division.
