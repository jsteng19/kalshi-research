Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu in Jerusalem, met with Romanian Foreign Minister Teodor Melescanu.

Prime Minister Netanyahu welcomed him and said that it was a sign of the long-standing and productive friendship between Romania and Israel. Romanian Foreign Minister Melescanu added that there was a natural partnership between the two countries.

The two discussed expanding bilateral cooperation in cyber, agriculture, technology and initiatives.
