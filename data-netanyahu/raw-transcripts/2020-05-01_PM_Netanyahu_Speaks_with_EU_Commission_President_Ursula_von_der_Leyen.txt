Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke by telephone with EU Commission President Ursula von der Leyen, who called to invite the Prime Minister to participate in the leaders' summit on dealing with the coronavirus. The leaders' summit will focus on contributing to the development of solutions for dealing with the coronavirus with emphasis on developing and producing a vaccine and developing drugs and equipment for patients. It is anticipated that leaders from around the world will attend, including from the G20 forum, the Gates Fund and other elements. The leaders will coordinate the global efforts and will cooperate among themselves and with the international agencies dealing with the matter. Prime Minister Netanyahu accepted the invitation and said that he would be happy to cooperate, contribute Israeli knowledge regarding the development of a vaccine and share insights on the steps needed to deal with the corona pandemic.
