Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today expressed his appreciation for US President Barack Obama's remarks at the

AIPAC Policy Conference

"I share the President's wish to promote peace and I appreciate his past and present efforts to achieve this goal. I am determined to work with President Obama in order to find ways to resume the peace negotiations. Peace is a vital necessity for us all," the Prime Minister said.
