Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, a short while ago today, spoke with Tel Aviv Sourasky Medical Center Director-General Prof. Gabriel Barbash, who is in Bulgaria.

Prof. Barbash informed Prime Minister Netanyahu that three people who were severely wounded in yesterday's terrorist attack have been flown to a hospital in Sofiam and that an additional 32 victims are currently en route to Israel. Another plane will take off from Bulgaria for Israel later. Prof. Barbash added that many people in Burgas have donated blood for the victims.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked Prof. Barbash for getting organized so quickly and asked him to convey his gratitude to the entire team.
