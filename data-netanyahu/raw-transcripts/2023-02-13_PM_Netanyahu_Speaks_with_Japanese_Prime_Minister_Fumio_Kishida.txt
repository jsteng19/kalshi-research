Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke_japan130223

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, spoke with Japanese Prime Minister Fumio Kishida, who expressed his condolences to the people of Israel for those who were murdered in the Jerusalem terrorist attacks. Japanese Prime Minister Kishida congratulated Prime Minister Netanyahu on assuming office. The two leaders discussed the importance of continuing to advance relations between Israel and Japan, especially the deepening of economic and security relations. The two leaders also praised the 70th anniversary of the establishment of relations between the two countries. Prime Minister Netanyahu invited Japanese Prime Minister Kishida to visit Israel.
