PM Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Norwegian Foreign Minister Ine Eriksen Soreide. The two discussed bilateral issues, regional issues and the situation in Gaza. It was agreed to expand cooperation in innovation with an emphasis on digital health. The two also discussed the upholding of the Gaza Reconstruction Mechanism by Norway through which it is verified that projects in Gaza serve their designated purpose ad not Hamas.
