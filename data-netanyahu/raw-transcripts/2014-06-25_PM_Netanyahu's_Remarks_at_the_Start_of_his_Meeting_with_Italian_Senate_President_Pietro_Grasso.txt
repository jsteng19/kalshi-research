Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today (Wednesday, 25 July 2014), at the start of his meeting with Italian Senate President Pietro Grasso, made the following remarks:

"I congratulate Public Security Minister Yitzhak Aharonovitch and Prison Service Commissioner Aharon Franko on their determined, professional and steadfast work in bringing an end to the prisoners' hunger-strike. We took a clear policy which led to this important result and we will now add other means so that there will be fewer prisoners' strikes in the future."
