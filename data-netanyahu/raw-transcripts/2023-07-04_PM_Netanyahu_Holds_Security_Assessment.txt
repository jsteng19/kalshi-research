Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, held a security assessment by teleconference. The Prime Minister was updated on the activity of the forces on the ground and briefed on the intelligence situation; future goals were set. Participating in the conversation were the Defense Minister, the Director of the ISA, the Deputy Director of the National Security Council, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Head of IDF Intelligence, the Head of IDF Operations, the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories and the Head of the IDF Intelligence Research Department.
