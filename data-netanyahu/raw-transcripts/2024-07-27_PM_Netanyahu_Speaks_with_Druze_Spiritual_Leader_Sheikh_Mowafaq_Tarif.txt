Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-conversation270724

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke earlier this evening with Sheikh Mowafaq Tarif and expressed his deep outrage over the murder of innocent people and children by Hezbollah. The Prime Minister conveyed heartfelt condolences on behalf of the entire people of Israel to the families of those who were murdered and to the entire Druze community. Prime Minister Netanyahu made it clear that Israel will not overlook this murderous attack and that Hezbollah will pay a heavy price which it has not paid up to now.
