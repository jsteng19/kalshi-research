Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this evening met with Russian National Security Council Secretary Nikolai Patrushev. The two men discussed the main strategic issues on the agenda and the strengthening of bilateral ties, including those between the two countries' National Security Councils.

The meeting was held in the context of Secy. Patrushev and his delegation's visit for the biannual meeting with Israel's National Security Council, chaired by Prof. Uzi Arad.

Secy. Patrushev also met with Defense Minister Ehud Barak and other senior security establishment officials.
