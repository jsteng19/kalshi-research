Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding a security assessment – at the Glilot Base at Camp Dayan – in the wake of today's events. Also participating in the discussion are the Defense Minister, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Director of the National Security Council, the Commander of the IDF Intelligence Branch, the Commander of the IDF Operations Branch, the Deputy Director of the ISA, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Head of IDF Southern Command and other senior IDF commanders.
