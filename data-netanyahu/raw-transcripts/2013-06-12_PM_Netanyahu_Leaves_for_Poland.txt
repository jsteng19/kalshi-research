Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu left on a working visit to Poland in the framework of the second meeting between the Israeli and polish governments. He will meet in Warsaw with Polish Prime Minister Donald Tusk while Israeli ministers meet with their Polish counterparts, to be followed by a joint Cabinet meeting.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and the ministers accompanying him will dedicate the new permanent exhibition 'Shoah' at Block 27 (the Jewish pavilion) at the Auschwitz-Birkenau State Museum. The pavilion was renovated by Yad Vashem; the Government of Israel invested NIS 30 million in the renovation and preservation process.
