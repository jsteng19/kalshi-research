Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Iowa Governor Kim Reynolds. At the start of the meeting, Gov. Reynolds signed a proclamation of support for the State of Israel on the occasion of 75 years of independence. Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked Gov. Reynolds for unreserved support for Israel. The two discussed economic cooperation, especially in agricultural technology. Also attending the meeting were the Director of the National Security Council, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the Director General of the Prime Minister's Office and the Prime Minister's Foreign Policy Adviser.
