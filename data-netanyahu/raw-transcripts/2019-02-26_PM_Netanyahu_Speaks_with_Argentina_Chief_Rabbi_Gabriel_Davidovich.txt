Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke by telephone with Argentina Chief Rabbi Gabriel Davidovich and wished him and his wife a quick recovery. The Prime Minister said: "First of all, on behalf of myself and all citizens of Israel and Jews, I would like to offer our support. I wish you both a full recovery. These wicked criminals should be found as soon as possible."
