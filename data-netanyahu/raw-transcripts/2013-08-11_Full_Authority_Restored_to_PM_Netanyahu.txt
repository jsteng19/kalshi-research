Full authority has been restored to Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu following a conversation between Hadassah Hospital-Ein Kerem Director Dr. Yuval, who examined him, and Cabinet Secretary Avihai Mandelblit.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said, "I would like to thank the professional and dedicated medical staff that took care of me, and all citizens who took an interest in my well-being. I continue to constantly see to your peace and security."
