Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, yesterday, held a series of conversations with world leaders: Cypriot President Nikos Christodoulides, British Prime Minister Rishi Sunak and Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese. Prime Minister Netanyahu updated the leaders on the war and thanked them for their unreserved support for the State of Israel. The Prime Minister said that Israel's war against Hamas-ISIS is also the war of the entire enlightened world.
