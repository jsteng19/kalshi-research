Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, met with Avichai Kremer, founder of the Prize4Life non-profit association, the goal of which is to accelerate research into Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) and assist those suffering from the disease. They discussed various ways to advance medical research, support patients and increase awareness of degenerative diseases.

In November 2011, Prime Minister Netanyahu awarded the Prime Minister's Prize for Initiatives and Innovation to Avichai for his work as the founder of Prize4Life.
