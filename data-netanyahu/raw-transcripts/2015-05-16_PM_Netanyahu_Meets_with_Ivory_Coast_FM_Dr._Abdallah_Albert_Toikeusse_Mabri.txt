Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, met with Ivory Coast Foreign Minister Dr. Abdallah Albert Toikeusse Mabri. The two discussed the global terrorism threat.

Prime Minister Netanyahu noted the importance that Israel ascribes to relations with Ivory Coast. He and the Ivory Coast Foreign Minister discussed opportunities for regional cooperation between Israel and the countries of western Africa.
