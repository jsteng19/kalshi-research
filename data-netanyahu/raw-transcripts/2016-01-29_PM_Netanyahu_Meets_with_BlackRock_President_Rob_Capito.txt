Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this morning in Jerusalem, met with

BlackRock President Rob Capito

in order to – inter alia – find new opportunities for additional international investments in Israel, which are important for the continued strengthening and growth of the Israeli economy.

BlackRock recently launched an exchange-traded fund (ETF) on the London Stock Exchange to allow European investors' access to the 25 largest companies on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange

. The fund views Israel as one of the world's leading technology centers.

Prime Minister's Office Director General Eli Groner and National Economic Council Chairman Prof. Avi Simhon also attended the meeting.

Last week, Prime Minister Netanyahu met in Davos with leaders of major international companies.
