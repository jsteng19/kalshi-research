Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke with Saeed Maasarwe, the father of Aya Maasarwe, the Israeli student who was murdered in Australia last week. Aya's coffin arrived in Israel this morning; she was laid to rest in Baqa al-Gharbiya.

Prime Minister Netanyahu offered his condolences to the family and said that he shared in its sorrow on behalf of all Israelis. Aya's father thanked him for his condolences and his support.
