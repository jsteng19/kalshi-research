Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, spoke with Prof. Nimrod Aloni and expressed his sorrow over the passing of former minister and MK Shulamit Aloni. "I send my condolences to the Aloni family. Despite the deep differences between us over the years, I appreciated her contribution to Israeli public life and her determination to stand up for the things that she believed in," the Prime Minister said.
