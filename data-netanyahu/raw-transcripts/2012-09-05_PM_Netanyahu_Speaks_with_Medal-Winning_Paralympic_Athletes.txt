Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, spoke with marksman Doron Shaziri, who won a silver medal in shooting at the London Paralympics, and with wheelchair doubles tennis players Noam Gershony and Shraga Weinberg, who won a bronze medal.

Prime Minister Netanyahu told Shaziri, "You are doing splendid work, congratulations to you. One needs the spirit of champions and you certainly have it." The Prime Minister told Gershony and Weinberg, "You have proven everything we said and thought about you. You are two champions joining other champions. For us, everything is gold. I wish you great success."
