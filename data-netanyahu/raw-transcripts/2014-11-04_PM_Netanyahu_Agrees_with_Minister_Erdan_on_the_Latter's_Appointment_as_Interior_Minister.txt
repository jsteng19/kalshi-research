Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu agreed with Minister Gilad Erdan that the latter would be appointed Interior Minister. They also agreed that Minister Erdan would continue to be responsible for the implementation of the reform at the Israel Broadcasting Authority, including the cancellation of the annual license fee. Minister Erdan will, in the coming weeks, conclude his tenure at the Communications Ministry. The foregoing will be submitted for Cabinet and Knesset approval.
