Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with a US Congressional delegation sponsored by the Yes! Israel Project (https://yes-israel.org/). Participating in the meeting were US Rep. Andrew Clyde (R-GA) and his wife Jennifer, and US Rep. Marlin Stutzman (R-IN) and his wife Christy.
