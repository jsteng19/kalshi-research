Minister Gilad Erdan has acceded to Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's proposal and will serve as Israeli Ambassador to the UN and Israeli Ambassador to the US. "I am proud and moved by the privilege of struggling for the justice of our cause in the international arena and defending Israel in the face of the expected challenges in the coming years," Minister Erdan said.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked Minister Erdan and said the he looks forward to continued cooperation between them on important missions for the State of Israel in the years ahead.

Minister Erdan will continue serving as a government minister until his appointment as ambassador is approved by the government.
