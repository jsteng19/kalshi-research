Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-condolences100523

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today:

"I was saddened to hear about the murder of two of our people in an attack at the entrance to the synagogue in Djerba in Tunisia, during Lag B'Omer celebrations.

On behalf of myself and the Government of Israel, I send heartfelt condolences to the families of those who were murdered. May their memories be for a blessing."
