Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today in Jerusalem, met with 26 Democratic members of the US Congress led by House Minority Whip Steny Hoyer.Prime Minister Netanyahu said that he was committed to peace: "The only way to achieve peace between us and the Palestinians is through direct negotiations. We are ready to begin negotiations without prior conditions immediately. If the UN recognizes a unilateral Palestinian step, his will harm the chance of achieving peace."Regarding Israel-Turkey relations, Prime Minister Netanyahu said that, "Israel neither wanted nor initiated the deterioration in relations with Turkey. We would like bilateral relations to return to their previous state."
