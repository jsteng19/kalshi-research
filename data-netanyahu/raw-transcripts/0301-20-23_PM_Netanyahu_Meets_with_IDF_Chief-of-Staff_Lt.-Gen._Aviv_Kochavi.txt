Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-meeting03012023

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, last night, met with IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Aviv Kochavi, who presented him with a wide-ranging intelligence briefing, as well as operational issues on the agenda and force-building measures. They also discussed at length the range of security matters regarding the State of Israel, and the efforts and operational matters for the future.
