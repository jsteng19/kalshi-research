Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, spoke with Israel Police Inspector General Roni Alsheich and ISA Director Nadav Argaman and thanked them for the exemplary operation to secure the funeral and the many VIPs who attended. The Prime Minister asked them to convey his gratitude and praise to their personnel.

Prime Minister Netanyahu also thanked everyone involved in organizing the ceremony: The National Information Directorate, the Government Press Office and the Information Center (Mercaz Ha'hasbara).
