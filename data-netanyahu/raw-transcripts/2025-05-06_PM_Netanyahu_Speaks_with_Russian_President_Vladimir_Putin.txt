Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening , spoke with Russian President Vladimir Putin. The two leaders exchanged warm greetings on the 80th anniversary of the victory over Nazi Germany in World War II.

The Prime Minister emphasized the contribution of the Red Army to the victory over the Nazis and stressed the role of the many Jewish commanders and fighters in the war.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and Russian President Putin also discussed regional developments.
