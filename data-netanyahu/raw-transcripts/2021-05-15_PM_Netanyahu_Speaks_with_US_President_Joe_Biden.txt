Prime Minister Benjamin, today, spoke with US President Joe Biden. The Prime Minister updated the President on developments and actions that Israel has taken and on actions that Israel intends to carry out. Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked President Biden for the US's unequivocal support of Israel's right to defend itself. Prime Minister Netanyahu emphasized that Israel is doing everything to avoid harming persons who are uninvolved; the proof of this is that the buildings in which terrorist targets are being hit by the IDF are evacuated of uninvolved persons.
