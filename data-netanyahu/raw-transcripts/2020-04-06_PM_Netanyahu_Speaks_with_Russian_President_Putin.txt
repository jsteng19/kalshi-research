Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today , spoke by telephone with Russian President Vladimir Putin and had a warm conversation in which President Putin wished him and the Israeli people a happy Passover and success in the struggle against the coronavirus.

The leaders discussed cooperation in the procurement of medical equipment and supplies. They also agreed to enable movement between Russia and Israel so that citizens of the two countries will be able to return home.
