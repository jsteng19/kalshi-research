Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, has instructed Cabinet Secretary Yossi Fuchs to contact the heads of the ultra-orthodox parties, leaders and opinion makers in a call to the public not to go to areas of friction or be dragged into provocations. Prime Minister Netanyahu praised the appeal by Rabbi Edelstein and the leaders of the ultra-orthodox sector who called on their public to act with tolerance and patience.
