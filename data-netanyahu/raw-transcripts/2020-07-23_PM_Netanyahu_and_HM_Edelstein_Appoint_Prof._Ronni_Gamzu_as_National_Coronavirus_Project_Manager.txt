Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Health Minister Yuli Edelstein, yesterday evening, decided to appoint Tel Aviv Sourasky Medical Center CEO Prof. Ronni Gamzu as national coronavirus project manager and as head of the control center. Prof. Gamzu has many years of administrative experience in the health field including previous service as Health Ministry Director General. At the onset of the coronavirus crisis, he established the 'Mothers and Fathers Shield' project to protect senior citizens from the coronavirus.
