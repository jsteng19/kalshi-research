Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Defense Minister Moshe Yaalon, visited the IDF soldiers who were wounded in yesterday's bomb explosion on the Golan Heights. They met with the parents of the soldier who was severely wounded and whose life is still in danger. Rambam Hospital Director Prof. Rafi Beyar updated the Prime Minister and the Defense Minister on the condition of the wounded and the treatment they are receiving.
