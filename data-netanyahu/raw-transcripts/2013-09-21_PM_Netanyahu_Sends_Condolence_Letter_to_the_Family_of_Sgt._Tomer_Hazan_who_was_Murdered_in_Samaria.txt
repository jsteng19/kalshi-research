Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, sent a condolence letter to the family of Sgt. Tomer Hazan from Bat Yam who was murdered in Samaria: "The pain of losing your dear son is difficult. The people of Israel share in your grief. This crime proves again that the struggle against terrorism is unceasing. We will continue to fight terrorism with all means at our disposal," the Prime Minister wrote.
