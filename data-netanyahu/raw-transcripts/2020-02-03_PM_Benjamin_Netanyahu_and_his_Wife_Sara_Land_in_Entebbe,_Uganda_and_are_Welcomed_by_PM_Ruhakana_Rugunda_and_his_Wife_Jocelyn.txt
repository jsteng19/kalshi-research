Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara, today , landed in Entebbe, Uganda and were welcomed by Prime Minister Ruhakana Rugunda and his wife Jocelyn. Prime Minister Netanyahu reviewed an honor guard; a local traditional dance was also performed. Prime Minister Netanyahu then went to the presidential residence for a meeting with Ugandan President Yoweri Museveni.
