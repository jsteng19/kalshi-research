Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, spoke with Palestinian Authority Chairman Mahmoud Abbas and offered his greetings on the occasion of the start of Ramadan.

"I called to greet you on the occasion of Ramadan. I hope that we will have an opportunity to speak to each other and not just on holidays, and that we begin negotiations. This is important. I hope that American Secretary of State John Kerry's efforts have results," the Prime Minister said.
