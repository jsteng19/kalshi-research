The Cabine, in a conference call, approved Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's proposal to extend the validity of regulatory permits until 30 September 2020. Due to the corona crisis, it was decided to extend the validity of all of the regulatory permits that expired between March and August, in order to make things easier for business owners. Prime Minister Netanyahu decided to use his authority and also submit for Cabinet approval the extension of the validity of the permits that are due to expire in the coming months.
