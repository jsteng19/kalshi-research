Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with the parents of the late Dor Chaim Nini and told them, "The people of Israel embrace you and share your pain. The enemies who are trying to expel us from here will not succeed in their goal."

Earlier, Prime Minister Netanyahu spoke with the family of the late Yohai Kalangel and told them, "I speak to you from the pain of bereavement. My heart grieves with you; your son fell in defense of our country."
