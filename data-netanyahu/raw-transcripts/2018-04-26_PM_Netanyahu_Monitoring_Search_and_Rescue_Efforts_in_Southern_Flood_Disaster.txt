Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke_south260418

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is closely monitoring the search and rescue efforts being carried out by the security forces in the flood disaster area in the south. The Prime Minister spoke by telephone with GOC Operations Maj.-Gen. Nitzan Alon and was updated by him on operations in the field.

"I commend the security and rescue forces that are now working to save lives and locate the missing in the terrible disaster that took place today in Nahal Tzafit. We are all praying for better news."
