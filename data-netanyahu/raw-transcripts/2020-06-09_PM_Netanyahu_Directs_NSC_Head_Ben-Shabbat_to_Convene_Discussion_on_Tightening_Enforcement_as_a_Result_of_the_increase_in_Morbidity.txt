Prime Minister Netanyahu spoke with Public Security Minister Amir Ohana and Interior Minister Aryeh Deri, and afterwards directed National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat to convene a discussion with the two ministers and with Prime Minister's Office Acting Director General Ronen Peretz, Health Ministry Director General Moshe Bar Siman Tov, Israel Police Acting Commissioner Moti Cohen, representatives of Attorney General Avichai Mandelblit and Federation of Local Authorities Chairman Haim Bibas in order to tighten enforcement as a result of the increase in morbidity.
