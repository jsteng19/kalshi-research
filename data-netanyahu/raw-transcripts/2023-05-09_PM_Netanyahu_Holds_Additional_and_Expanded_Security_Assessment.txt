Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, held an additional and expanded security assessment, ahead of the meeting of the Security Cabinet later today at the Kirya in Tel Aviv. Participating in the discussion were the Defense Minister, the Strategic Affairs Minister, the Director of the National Security Council, the Director of the Mossad, the Director of the ISA, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the Director General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Head of the Mossad Intelligence Branch, the Head of the IDF Intelligence Branch, the Head of the IDF Operations Branch, the Head of the IDF Strategic Branch, the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories, the Head of the IDF Intelligence Branch Research Division, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary and the Defense Minister's Military Secretary.
