Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today (Saturday), 12.12.09, strongly condemns the attack on the mosque in Kfar Yasuf and has ordered the security services to act determinedly in order to apprehend the perpetrators and bring them to justice as quickly as possible.

The Prime Minister reiterated that there is no place for violence of any sort, neither Jews against Palestinians nor Palestinians against Jews, and added that the Government will act aggressively against any and all displays of violence.
