Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today thanked German Chancellor Angela Merkel for the agreement to provide Israel with an additional submarine. In a letter that he sent to Chancellor Merkel, Prime Minister Netanyahu wrote: “I would like to thank you, both personally and on behalf of the Government of Israel, for your agreement to provide Israel with an additional submarine. This step will help us meet Israel’s considerable security needs in these stormy times and will, over time, greatly contribute to the security of the Jewish state.”
