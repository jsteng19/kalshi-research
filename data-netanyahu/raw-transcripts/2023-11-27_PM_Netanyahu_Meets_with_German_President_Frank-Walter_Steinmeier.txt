Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at his Knesset office, met with German President Frank-Walter Steinmeier and commended him for visiting the country and for being a true friend of Israel.

The Prime Minister thanked the German President for Germany's consistent support for Israel's obligation to defend itself, and stressed that the international community must stand alongside Israel in its war against the Hamas-ISIS terrorist organization.
