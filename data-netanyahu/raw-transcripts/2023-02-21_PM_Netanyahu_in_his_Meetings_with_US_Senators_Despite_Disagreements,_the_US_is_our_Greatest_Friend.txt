Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, met with US Senator Jim Risch (R-ID), the Ranking Member of the Committee on Foreign Relations. Earlier today, the Prime Minister met with US Senator Alex Padilla (D-CA). Both meetings were held in the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem. In his meetings, Prime Minister Netanyahu stressed the importance of the link between Israel and the US and said that despite disagreements, the US is our greatest friend. These meetings are an expression of the preservation of the bipartisan ties with Israel. Among the topics discussed were the Abraham Accords and their possible expansion, as well as confronting Iran and its proxies.
