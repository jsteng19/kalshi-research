Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, at the dinner at the President's Residence in Jerusalem, has met with dozens of leaders. Among those he has spoken with so far are the King of Spain, the President of Austria, the President of France, the President of Italy, the President of Iceland, the President of Armenia, the President of Bosnia-Herzegovina, the President of Germany, the Prime Minister of Denmark, the President of Greece, the President of the European Council, the President of Macedonia, the President of Serbia, the Prime Minister of the Czech Republic, the President of Cyprus, the President of Romania and many others.
