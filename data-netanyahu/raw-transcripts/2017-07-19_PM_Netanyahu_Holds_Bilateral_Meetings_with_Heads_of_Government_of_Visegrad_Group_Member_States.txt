Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, held a series of bilateral meetings with the heads of government of the Visegard Group: Czech Prime Minister Bohuslav Sobotka, Polish Prime Minister Beata Szydlo and Slovakian Prime Minister Robert Fico. (Prime Minister Netanyahu met yesterday with Hungarian Prime Minister Viktor Orban.)

At his meeting with Czech Prime Minister Sobotka, Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked him for his warm concern for Israel and for his support in international forums, and praised the recent decision by the Czech parliament regarding Jerusalem. Czech Prime Minister Sobotka invited Prime Minister Netanyahu to visit Prague at the earliest possible opportunity. Prime Minister Netanyahu accepted the invitation.
