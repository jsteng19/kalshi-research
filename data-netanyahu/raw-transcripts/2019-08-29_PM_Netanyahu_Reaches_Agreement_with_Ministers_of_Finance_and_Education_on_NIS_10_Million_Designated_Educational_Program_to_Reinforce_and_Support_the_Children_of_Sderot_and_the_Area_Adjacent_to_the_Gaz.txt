Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, reached agreement with Finance Minister Moshe Kahlon and Education Minister Rafi Peretz on a NIS 10 million designated educational program to reinforce and support the children of Sderot and the area adjacent to the Gaza Strip in the coming school year. The details of the program will be formulated by a team led by Prime Minister's Office Acting Director General Ronen Peretz, Education Ministry Director General Shmuel Abuav and Finance Ministry Budget Director Shaul Meridor.
