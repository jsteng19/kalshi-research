Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/eventtonga070812

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu met with Tonga PM Lord Tu'ivakano, who is currently visiting Israel. Tonga Health Minister Lord Tu'i'afitu and Education, Women's Affairs and Culture Minister Dr. Hon. 'Ana Taufe'ulungaki, and Deputy Health Minister Yaakov Litzman also attended the meeting.

The prime ministers discussed bilateral relations and the possibility of expanding cooperation in the fields of public health and solar energy.
