Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, via his special envoy Yitzhak Molcho, sent a message to Palestinian Authority Chairman Mahmoud Abbas and PA Prime Minister Salam Fayyad.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that last night's incident near Bat Ayin was very grave and added that the security forces are making extensive efforts to locate those responsible in order to bring them to justice. He said that he would monitor the medical care that the wounded are receiving in Israel and offered all additional assistance.
