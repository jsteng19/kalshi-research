Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/event_usa181220

Prime Minister Netanyahu spoke via videoconference with the US Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Gen. Mark A. Milley, in the framework of his visit to Israel, and later met with him after leaving quarantine. The two discussed regional threats, especially the Iranian aggression. They also discussed cooperation between the IDF and the US Armed Forces, and opportunities in the region.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked US Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Gen. Milley for the cooperation that has expanded and deepened in recent years under the Trump administration and expressed his willingness to continue deepening the cooperation.
