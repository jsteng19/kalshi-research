Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, spoke by telephone with UN Secretary General Antonio Guterres.

The Prime Minister said that he views with utmost gravity the flagrant violation of Israeli sovereignty and the violation of UN Security Council Resolution #1701. He added that this was an additional part of Iran's aggression in the region.

Prime Minister Netanyahu updated UNSG Guterres on the details of Operation Northern Shield and said that he expects the UN to strongly condemn the violation of Israel's sovereignty.

The Prime Minister also told the UN Secy.-Gen. that the international community must join in the demand to impose increased sanctions on Hezbollah in the wake of the events.
