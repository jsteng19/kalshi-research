Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Finance Minister Bezalel Smotrich, this afternoon, held a preparatory discussion for the 2023-2024 state budget. Prime Minister Netanyahu and Finance Minister Smotrich thanked the professional staff at the Finance Ministry for their hard work in recent weeks to prepare the budget. Also participating in the discussion were the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff Tzachi Braverman, Prime Minister's Office Director General Yossi Shelley, Finance Ministry Director General Shlomi Heizler, National Economic Council Director Prof. Avi Simhon, Bank of Israel Governor Amir Yaron, Chief Economist Shira Greenberg, Accountant General Yaheli Rothenberg, Finance Ministry Budget Director Yogev Gardos, Tax Authority Director Eran Yaacov and other senior officials.
