Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke-discussion300523

The discussion initiated by Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu – with the participation of Finance Minister Bezalel Smotrich, Agriculture Minister Avi Dichter and the professional teams – on the fight against the cost of living, has concluded. Among the topics discussed were the urgent steps that are needed in the fight against the cost of living. It was decided that Finance Minister Smotrich, in coordination with Economy and Industry Minister Nir Barkat, would submit measures on the opening of the market, the cancellation of cartelization and lowering regulation, together with a timetable for their implementation.
