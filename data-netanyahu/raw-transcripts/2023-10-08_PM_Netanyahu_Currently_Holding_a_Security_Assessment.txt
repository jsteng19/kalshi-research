Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding a security assessment at the Kirya in Tel Aviv.

The Defense Minister, the Strategic Affairs Minister, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Director of the ISA, the Director of the Mossad, the Director of the National Security Council, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Head of IDF Intelligence, the Head of IDF Operations, the Head of the IDF Strategic Branch, the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories and the Head of Research at IDF Intelligence, participated in the discussion.
