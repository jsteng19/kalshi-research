Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, convened an assessment of the situation regarding events in Russia. The Prime Minister said, at the end of the discussion, that while this was an internal Russian matter in which Israel was not involved, Israel would continue to monitor developments closely. Also participating in the discussion were the Defense Minister, the Foreign Minister, the Aliyah and Integration Minister, the Strategic Affairs Minister, the Director of the National Security Council, the Director of the ISA, the Director of the Mossad, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Defense Ministry Director General, the Chairman of the Jewish Agency and other officials.
