Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, together with Air Force Commander Maj.-Gen. Tomer Bar and the Air Defense Array Commander, visited an Arrow Unit, at an Air Force base.

At the start of the visit, the Prime Minister received an operational briefing. He then entered the intercept control center, where the Air Defense Array Commander presented the missions of the array as well as the operational challenges it faces.

Prime Minister Netanyahu commended the array's soldiers and officers and expressed his appreciation for their contribution in defending the skies over Israel.
