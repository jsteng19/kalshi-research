Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke by phone a short while ago with German Chancellor Angela Merkel. The conversation followed the Chancellor's announcement that she will not run in the next election.

The Prime Minister expressed his appreciation for Merkel's contribution to Israel's security and thanked her for their joint work.

Prime Minister Netanyahu: "We will continue to work together to further our common objectives".
