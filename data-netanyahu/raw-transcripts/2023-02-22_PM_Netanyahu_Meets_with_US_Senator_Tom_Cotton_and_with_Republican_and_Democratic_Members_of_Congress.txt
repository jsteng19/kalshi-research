Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, held a series of diplomatic meetings. He first met this morning with US Senator Tom Cotton (R-AR), then met with a bipartisan Congressional delegation and afterwards with a Democratic Congressional delegation.

Prime Minister Netanyahu emphasized the importance of the bipartisan ties between Israel and the US and thanked his visitors for their support for Israel.

Among the topics discussed were regional developments, particularly Iran and its proxies in the Middle East as well as the great contribution of the Abraham Accords to regional stability and possibilities for their expansion.
