Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu had a phone conversation this morning with Massachusetts Governor Deval Patrick, who is visiting Israel this week as head of a delegation comprising businessmen and women and researchers in the field of cyber, hi tech and the life sciences. The Prime Minister and the Governor discussed the importance of the academic and commercial ties between Israel and Massachusetts, as well as ways to advance the joint cooperation in the field of innovation and research. The Prime Minister and the Governor emphasized the cyber industry and the potential for joint development in this area.
