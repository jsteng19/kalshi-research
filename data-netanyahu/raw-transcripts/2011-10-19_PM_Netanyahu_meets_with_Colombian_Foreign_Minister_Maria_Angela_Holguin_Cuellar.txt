Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today in Jerusalem, met with Colombian Foreign Minister María Angela Holguín Cuéllar and noted the deep ties between Israel and Colombia. He thanked her for Colombia's opposition to the unilateral establishment of a Palestinian state. "I am pleased that you also think that peace is achieved through direct negotiations without pre-conditions," the Prime Minister said.Prime Minister Netanyahu told the Colombian Foreign Minister about his emotional meeting yesterday with soldier Gilad Shalit and noted the resemblance between Shalit's captivity and that of Colombian presidential candidate Ingrid Betancourt Pulecio. The Prime Minister added that today he was happy to see photographs of Gilad walking together with his mother outside their home and said that, "I am pleased to see that Gilad is beginning to get back to normality."
