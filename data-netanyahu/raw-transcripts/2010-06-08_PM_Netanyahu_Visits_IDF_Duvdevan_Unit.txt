Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today (Tuesday), 8.6.10, visited the IDF Duvdevan unit. The Prime Minister spoke with the unit's soldiers and commanders and told them: "Today, Israel's very right to defend itself is under attack. You embody this right. You have proven that there is no limit to your amazing abilities and expertise and I salute you."
