Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has concluded an extended meeting, in his office, with US Secretary of State Marco Rubio and his team.

Among those attending the meeting were Minister of Foreign Affairs Gideon Saar, Strategic Affairs Minister Ron Dermer, National Security Council Director Tzachi Hanegbi, US Ambassador to Israel Mike Huckabee and Israeli Ambassador to Israel Yechiel Leiter.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and Secretary of State Rubio will issue statements shortly.
