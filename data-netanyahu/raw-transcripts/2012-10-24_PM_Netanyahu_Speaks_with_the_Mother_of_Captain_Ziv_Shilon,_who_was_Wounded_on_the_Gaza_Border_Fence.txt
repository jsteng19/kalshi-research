Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon spoke with Margalit Shilon, the mother of Captain Ziv Shilon who was wounded on the Gaza border fence.

The Prime Minister wished Ziv a rapid recovery and said, "There is nothing like a son's love for his mother. Today, the entire nation loves and embraces your son. We will do our utmost to assist his rehabilitation."
