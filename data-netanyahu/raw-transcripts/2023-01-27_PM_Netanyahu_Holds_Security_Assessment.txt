Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today (Thursday, 26 January 2023), following a security assessment, expressed his appreciation for the bravery and resourcefulness of the soldiers who thwarted terrorist attacks that could have cost many lives.

The Prime Minister received detailed updates from the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Director of the ISA, the Israel Police Inspector General and the Commander of the IDF Intelligence Branch, together with the Defense Minister, the National Security Minister, the Foreign Minister, the Director of the National Security Council and the Prime Minister's Military Secretary.

Prime Minister Netanyahu made it clear that while Israel is not seeking escalation, he has instructed the security forces to prepare for all scenarios in the various sectors in order to safeguard the citizens of Israel.
