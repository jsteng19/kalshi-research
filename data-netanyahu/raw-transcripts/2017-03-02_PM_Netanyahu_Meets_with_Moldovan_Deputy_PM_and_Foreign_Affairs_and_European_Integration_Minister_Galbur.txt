Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, met with Moldovan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Affairs and European Integration Minister Andrei Galbur. The Prime Minister noted the excellent relations between the two countries and said that this was a welcome and stable friendship.

Prime Minister Netanyahu:

"We are working together and cooperate in all the areas that are of interest to you: Agriculture, water and medicine and also in the workers that come here to help build Israel from Moldova. We have had, of course, a relationship that spans centuries and the important thing is now that we have our efforts to seize the future together."
