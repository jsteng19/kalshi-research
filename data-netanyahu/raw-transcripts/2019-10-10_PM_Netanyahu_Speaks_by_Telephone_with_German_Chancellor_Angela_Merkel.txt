Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke by telephone with German Chancellor Angela Merkel and expressed his appreciation for her vigorous stand against anti-semitism. The Prime Minister said that it was important to step up efforts against manifestations of anti-semitism and the Chancellor agreed. Chancellor Merkel pointed out that she also intended to increase efforts to provide for the security of the Jewish community.
