Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara have arrived in Australia, following their visit to Singapore. The Prime Minister will meet later today (Wednesday, 22 February 2017; Australia time) with Australian Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull and with Israeli and Australian business people. Afterwards, he will attend a large event with Australian Jewish community leaders at the Central Synagogue in Sydney.
