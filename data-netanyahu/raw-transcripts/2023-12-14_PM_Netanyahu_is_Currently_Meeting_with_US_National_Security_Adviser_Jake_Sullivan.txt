Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently meeting with US National Security Adviser Jake Sullivan at the Kirya in Tel Aviv. Also participating in the meeting are National Security Adviser and Director of the National Security Council Tzachi Hanegbi and US President Joe Biden's Special Envoy to the Middle East Brett McGurk.

An expanded meeting with the members of the War Cabinet will be held later.
