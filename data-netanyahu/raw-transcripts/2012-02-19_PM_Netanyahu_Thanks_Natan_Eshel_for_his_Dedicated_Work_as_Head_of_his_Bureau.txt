Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu would like to thank Natan Eshel for his dedicated and good work over the years as head of the Prime Minister's Bureau, and to commend him for his important contribution to the successful work of the Government.

Attorney General Yehuda Weinstein today updated Prime Minister Netanyahu on the details of the plea bargain and noted that the personnel of his Bureau acted according to the law, as is expected of them.

Prime Minister Netanyahu asked Gil Sheffer to serve in Natan Eshel's place as head of the Bureau in order to ensure continuity of the work.
