PM Netanyahu Meets with Delegation of US Ambassadors in Europe Led by US Ambassador to Israel David Friedman

Prime Minister Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with a delegation of US ambassadors in Europe led by US Ambassador to Israel David Friedman. The Prime Minister updated the delegation on the security situation, said that the State of Israel had the right to defend itself and added that we would take all necessary measures to ensure this. Prime Minister Netanyahu said that the Hamas terrorist organization was committing a double war crime by firing indiscriminately on civilians from among concentrations of civilians. Participating in the meeting were delegation head US Ambassador to Israel David Friedman, US Ambassador to France Jamie D. McCourt, US Ambassador to the EU Gordon Sondland, US Ambassador to Germany Richard Grenell, US Ambassador to Portugal George Edward Glass and US Special Envoy to Monitor and Combat Anti-Semitism Elan S. Carr.
