Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke with President Reuven Rivlin and expressed his sorrow on the passing of his sister Esther (Eti) Herling.

The Prime Minister said he knew that Esther, who was born in Jerusalem and had fought in the Irgun, was very dear to the President and expressed his support at this difficult time.
