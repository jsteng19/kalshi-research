Finance Minister Yisrael Katz, Deputy Minister Fateen Mulla and Prime Minister’s Office Acting Director General Ronen Peretz met last night, at the behest of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, with Druze spiritual leader Sheikh Muafak Tarif and the committee of Druze community leaders in order to formulate an additional five-year plan to develop and strengthen the Druze and Circassian communities. It was agreed that the Prime Minister’s Office, Finance Ministry and Regional Cooperation Ministry would begin immediately to formulate a five-year plan to develop and strengthen Druze and Circassian towns, which will be budgeted within the framework of the state budget. It was also agreed that the ministries would continue to work to expedite the government decision to appropriate NIS 200 million, which was approved during the transition government period. Forum of Druze and Circassian Local Authorities Chairman Jabr Hamoud, Druze community leaders, Finance Ministry Budget Director Shaul Meridor, Prime Minister’s Office Deputy Director General for Home Affairs, Planning and Development Lior Farber and other officials also attended the meeting.
