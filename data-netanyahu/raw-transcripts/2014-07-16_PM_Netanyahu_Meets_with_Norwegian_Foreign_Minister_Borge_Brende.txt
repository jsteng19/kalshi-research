Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu in Jerusalem, met with Norwegian Foreign Minister Borge Brende and told him that Israel has proven to the international community that it is ready to consider additional ways apart from expanded military action. But he noted that since Hamas rejected the Egyptian proposal , it would bear the consequences of its decision.

Prime Minister added that the international community needs to support the demilitarization of Gaza from rockets and tunnels.
