Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu asked Minister Ofir Akunis to evaluate possibilities for financing the Israel Broadcasting Authority (which is being dismantled) until the broadcasts of the public broadcasting corporation are on the air, as well as sources of financing for original productions, until the same date, and to submit his recommendations to him.

Prime Minister Netanyahu also asked Minister Akunis to evaluate the 2009 arrangement, which was determined pursuant to the 1990 Second Authority for Television and Radio Law, regarding the transition from franchises to licenses, as well as the amended 2014 Second Authority for Television and Radio Law (as approved by the Ministerial Committee on Legislation on 26 October 2014), and to submit his recommendations to him.
