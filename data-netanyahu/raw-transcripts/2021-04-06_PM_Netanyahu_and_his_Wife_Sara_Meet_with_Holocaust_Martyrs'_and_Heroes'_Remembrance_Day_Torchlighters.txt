Prime Minister Netanyahu and his wife Sara, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with the torchlighters for Holocaust Martyrs' and Heroes' Remembrance Day. The Prime Minister and his wife heard the moving and brave stories of the six Holocaust survivors who will light the torches at the state ceremony tomorrow evening at Yad Vashem, and of Roza Bloch who will address the ceremony on behalf of the survivors.
