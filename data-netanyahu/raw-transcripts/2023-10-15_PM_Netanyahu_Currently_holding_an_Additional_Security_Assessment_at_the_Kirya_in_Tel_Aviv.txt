Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding an additional security assessment at the Kirya in Tel Aviv. Attending the discussion are the Defense Minister, the Strategic Affairs Minister, Minister Benny Gantz, Minister Gadi Eisenkot, MK Aryeh Deri, the Director of the National Security Council, the Director of the Mossad, the Director of the ISA, the Prime Minister's Chief of Staff, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Prime Minister's Military Secretary, the Cabinet Secretary, the Head of Mossad Intelligence, the Head of IDF Intelligence, the Head of IDF Operations, the Head of the IDF Strategic Branch, the Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories and the Head of Research at IDF Intelligence.
