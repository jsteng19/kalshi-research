PM Netanyahu Meets with Hungarian Security Policy and Foreign Policy Advisor Jozsef Czukor

PM Netanyahu met with Hungarian Prime Minister Viktor Orban's security policy and foreign policy advisor, Jozsef Czukor. The meeting was in continuation of Prime Minister Netanyahu's participation in the Visegrad Group summit on 19 July 2017 in Budapest, where he offered to host the summit in Israel.

Earlier Czukor met with National Security Council Chairman and National Security Advisor Meir Ben-Shabbat. The two discussed regional developments, counter-terrorism cooperation, deepening bilateral diplomatic-security relations and various other bilateral issues.
