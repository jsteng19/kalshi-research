Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara, at the Prime Minister's Residence in Jerusalem, hosted Polish President Andrzej Duda and his wife, Agata Kornhauser-Duda, for dinner. The Prime Minister and his wife showed the Polish President and his wife the archaeological items on display at the Residence which show the link between the Jewish People and the Land of Israel.
