Source: https://www.gov.il/en/pages/spoke_tira160520

Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening: "I strongly condemn the shooting incident in Tira that was carried out against police officers while they were acting against crime there. I send my best wishes for a quick recovery to the police officers who were wounded in the incident. We will continue to act and fight crime and violence in the Arab sector and we will deal with the criminals to the fullest extent of the law."
