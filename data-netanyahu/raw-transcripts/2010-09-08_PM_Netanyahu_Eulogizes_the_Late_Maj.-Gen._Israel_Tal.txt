Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu expresses his deep sorrow over the passing of

Maj.-Gen. (ret.) Israel Tal

. The Prime Minister pointed out that Tal was one of the pillars of the IDF and the State of Israel's national security. The developer of the Merkava tank, Tal was an admired commander in battle and one of the architects of the IDF. He was entirely dedicated to Israel 's security and to ensuring the future of the people of Israel .
