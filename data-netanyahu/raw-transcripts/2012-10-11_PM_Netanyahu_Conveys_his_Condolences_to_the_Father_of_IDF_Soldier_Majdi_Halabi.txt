Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke a short time ago, today (Thursday, 11 October 2012), with Nazmi Halabi, the father of IDF soldier Majdi Halabi, and conveyed his condolences to the Halabi family and the Druze community. "My heart is with you. I understand the deep pain of receiving the awful announcement," the Prime Minister said.
