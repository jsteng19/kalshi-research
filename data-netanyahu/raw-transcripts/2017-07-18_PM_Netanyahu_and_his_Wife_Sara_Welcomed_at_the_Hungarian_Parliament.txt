Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara were welcomed at the Hungarian Parliament in Budapest by Hungarian Prime Minister Viktor Orban and his wife Aniko Levai. Prime Minister Netanyahu reviewed an honor guard composed of dozens of soldiers. A military band played the Israeli and Hungarian national anthems. The prime ministers and their wives then toured Parliament.

The two prime ministers are currently meeting. Afterwards they will hold an expanded meeting – with Hungarian ministers and Prime Minister Netanyahu's entourage – during which a bilateral culture agreement and declarations of intent regarding cooperation in innovation and technology will be signed.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and Hungarian Prime Minister Orban will issue statements after the expanded meeting.
