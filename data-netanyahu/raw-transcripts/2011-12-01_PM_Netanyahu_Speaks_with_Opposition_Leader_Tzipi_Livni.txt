Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, told Leader of the Opposition MK Tzipi Livni that if the Palestinians want to negotiate, the only way to do so is with Israel's elected Government.

Prime Minister Netanyahu made the foregoing remarks to MK Livni in a telephone call in which she updated him on her meeting last night with Palestinian Authority Chairman Mahmoud Abbas.
