Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, together with Finance Minister Yisrael Katz and Education Minister Yoav Galant, have secured, via a conference call, Cabinet approval for a decision to allocate NIS 300 million to subsidize afternoon daycare until the end of the 2020-2021 school year. The decision will allow for the continuation of the Education Ministry subsidy for afternoon educational frameworks, which provide academic, social and educational enrichment activities, assistance with homework preparation and a hot, nutritious meal. The ministers approved the continuation of the subsidy given to these frameworks until the end of the 2020-2021 school year in order to continue to provide solutions for parents - to encourage employment in the economy and reduce the cost of living, especially while dealing with the corona crisis.
