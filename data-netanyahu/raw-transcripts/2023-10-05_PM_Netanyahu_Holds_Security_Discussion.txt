Following the recent terrorist attacks in Judea and Samaria, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening (Thursday, 5 October 2023), held a discussion with the participation of the Defense Minister, the IDF Chief-of-Staff, the Director of the ISA, the Head of IDF Central Command and the Prime Minister's Military Secretary.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that terrorist attacks in Judea and Samaria are unacceptable and directed the IDF and ISA to increase defensive and preventive measures against terrorists while focusing immediately on the Hawara corridor.

The Prime Minister referred to the pace of work on paving the Hawara bypass road and ordered that the remaining construction time until the opening of the road be shortened by half.

Prime Minister Netanyahu instructed the IDF and the security services to present him with additional actions in the fight against terrorism and to increase security for the residents of Judea and Samaria. The discussion will be held on Sunday morning.
