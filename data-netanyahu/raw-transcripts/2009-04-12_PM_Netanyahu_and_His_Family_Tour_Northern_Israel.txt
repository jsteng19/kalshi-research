Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, his wife Sarah and their two sons, Yair and Avner, toured

Israel Nature and National Parks Protection Authority

and heritage sites in northern Israel.

The tour began at Tel Kedesh in the upper Galilee and continued on to

Nebi Yusha

Tel Hazor

and the ancient synagogue at

Corazim

. The Prime Minister and his family concluded their tour at

Kfar Nahum (Capernaum)

, from which they observed the Golan Heights. They defined their trip as, "A tour of the deep roots of Jewish history."

At Prime Minister Netanyahu's request, the routine of those visiting the various sites was not disturbed; the Prime Minister and his family visited the sites along with the other visitors.
