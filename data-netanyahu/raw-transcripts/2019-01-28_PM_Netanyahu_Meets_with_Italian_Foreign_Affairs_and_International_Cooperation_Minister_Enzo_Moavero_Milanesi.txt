PM Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Italian Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation Minister Enzo Moavero Milanesi. The two discussed bilateral cooperation in technology, innovation and industry, and the East Med natural gas pipeline that is due to export Israeli gas via Cyprus and Greece to other countries in Europe.

Prime Minister Netanyahu briefed his guest on Iran's efforts to entrench itself militarily in Syria and its actions to undermine stability in the Middle East
