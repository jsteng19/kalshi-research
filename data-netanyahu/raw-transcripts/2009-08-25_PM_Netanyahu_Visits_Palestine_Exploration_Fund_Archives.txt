Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife this morning, in a small basement in London , visited the

Palestine Exploration Fund

archives, and viewed various rare archaeological treasures documenting the scenic vistas and history of the land of Israel in the last quarter of the 19th century.

Prime Minister Netanyahu proposed to cooperate with the Fund so as to enable an exhibition in Israel of its rarities and instructed Cabinet Secretary Tzvi Hauser to promote the initiative. "It is important that the Israeli public be exposed to these rare treasures, which are hidden away in an archive in London , and which document a fascinating chapter in the history of the Land of Israel ," the Prime Minister said.
