Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with German Federal Minister for Foreign Affairs Heiko Maas. The Prime Minister commended the successful G2G in Israel with German Chancellor Angela Merkel and ministers in her government.

Prime Minister Netanyahu reiterated that Israel expects the international community to stand against Iran's aggression in Syria and Lebanon. He also made it clear that Israel would take strong action against Gaza for every action against Israel and the residents of the south.
