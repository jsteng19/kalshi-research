Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke from his plane with Defense Minister Ehud Barak, IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Gaby Ashkenazi and ISA Director Yuval Diskin and was updated on the murderous terrorist attack. The Prime Minister directed the security services to act without any political restrictions in order to reach the murderers and to act aggressively against those who dispatched them.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that, "My heart goes out to those who were murdered and their families. I would like to convey a message of solidarity and support to all of our brothers and sisters in Judea and Samaria ."
