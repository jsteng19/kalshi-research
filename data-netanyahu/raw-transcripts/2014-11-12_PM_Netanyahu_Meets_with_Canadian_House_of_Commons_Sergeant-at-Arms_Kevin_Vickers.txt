Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Knesset, met with Canadian House of Commons Sergeant-at-Arms Kevin M. Vickers, who shot the terrorist that perpetrated the attack at the Canadian Parliament building in Ottawa on 22 October. Vickers told how he neutralized the terrorist who had opened fire in the Parliament building and had shot several people, killing one.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that the terrorist attack in Ottawa proved once again that Islamic extremist terrorism knows no borders. He added that Israel and Canada would continue to fight the forces of global terrorism.
