Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today , convened a discussion with Health Minister Yuli Edelstein, Deputy Health Minister Yoav Kisch, National Security Council head Meir Ben-Shabbat, Health Ministry Director General Prof. Chezy Levy, national coronavirus project manager Prof. Nachman Ash and other senior officials. The discussion focused on analyzing morbidity trends, the pace of vaccinations, the effect of the mutations on morbidity, target dates for the exit from the lockdown and other issues. The senior Health Ministry officials expressed concern that even at the end of the current lockdown we will remain with a high level of morbidity and very heavy crowding of severe cases in the hospitals. It was agreed that consultations would continue tomorrow.
