The Cabinet, at its weekly meeting today, approved Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's proposal to establish a ministerial committee on civilian emergency preparedness, including earthquakes. The Prime Minister instructed that an updated national plan on home front preparedness and dealing with earthquakes be presented at the committee's first meeting, in which he will also participate.
