Prime Minister Netanyahu at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with US Senator (R-SC) Lindsey Graham. Prime Minister Netanyahu: "No one has done more for Israel than you, Senator Lindsey Graham, a stalwart champion of our alliance and we have no better friend. I want to thank you for everything you've been doing over the years on our defenses, our security, on Iran. You've been a tremendous friend and a tremendous ally."
