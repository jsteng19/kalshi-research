Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today spoke to Egyptian Supreme Military Council Chairman Field Marshal Mohamed Hussein Tantawi and thanked him – on behalf of the State of Israel – for Egypt's central role in achieving the agreement for the return of captive soldier Gilad Shalit.

Prime Minister Netanyahu stressed the intensive and successful effort that Egypt has invested in recent months and told Field Marshal Tantawi that, "Your assistance warms the heart of every Israeli."
