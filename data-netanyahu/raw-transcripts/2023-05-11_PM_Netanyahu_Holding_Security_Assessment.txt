In wake of recent developments, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is currently holding a security assessment, by teleconference, at the Kirya in Tel Aviv.

Participating in the assessment are Defense Minister Yoav Gallant, Strategic Affairs Minister Ron Dermer, Prime Minister's Chief of Staff Tzachi Braverman, National Security Council Director Tzachi Hanegbi, ISA Director Ronen Bar, IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Herzi Halevi, Prime Minister's Office Director General Yossi Shelley, Prime Minister's Military Secretary Maj.-Gen. Avi Gil, Head of IDF Operations Maj.-Gen. Oded Basiuk, Head of IDF Intelligence Maj.-Gen. Aharon Haliva and Coordinator of Government Activities in the Territories Maj.-Gen. Ghasan Alyan.
