Prime Minister Netanyahu spoke with Bahraini Crown Prince Salman bin Hamad Al Khalifa; the two discussed the strengthening of bilateral relations.

Prime Minister Netanyahu after the conversation:

"I just spoke with Bahraini Crown Prince Salman bin Hamad Al Khalifa. This was our second conversation; it was very friendly.

Both of us are very moved by the fact that we can bring peace to our peoples and our countries in a very short time. Therefore, he also invited me to make an official visit to Bahrain soon. I will do so, on your behalf, with pleasure."
