Prime Minister Netanyahu and Coordinator for Captives and MIAs Yaron Bloom, at the Prime Minister’s Office in Jerusalem, met with the Mengistu and A-Said families. Prime Minister Netanyahu told the families that he is committed to bringing back home the soldiers and civilians being held by Hamas. He updated them on the latest developments and the open and covert efforts being made by the State of Israel to return the boys. Also attending the meeting were National Security Council Chairman Meir Ben-Shabbat, the Prime Minister’s Military Secretary Brig.-Gen. Avi Bluth and a Foreign Ministry representative. Earlier this week, Prime Minister Netanyahu met with the Goldin and Shaul families.
