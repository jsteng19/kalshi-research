Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu said that the State of Israel mourns the passing of its fourth President, Prof. Ephraim Katzir. One of Israel's outstanding scientists, Ephraim Katzir was completely committed to the State of Israel. Prof. Katzir was among the first and most prominent scientists at the Weizmann Institute of Science and also contributed to Israel's security. Ephraim Katzir's integrity and modesty were an example for all.
