Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this morning, was the guest of Tel Aviv-Jaffa Mayor Ron Huldai. The two men toured the Sarona area in Tel Aviv, including the Mill House museum and the old winery. The Prime Minister met and spoke with workers and visitors and was received warmly.

Afterwards, Prime Minister Netanyahu went to the Municipality building where he and Mayor Huldai took in the view from the rooftop observation deck. Their subsequent working meeting focused on several issues including tourism and hotels, transportation, the economy and commerce.
