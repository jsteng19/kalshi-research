Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke with Brazilian President Jair Bolsonaro following the dam collapse disaster in Brazil. The Prime Minister offered that Israel would immediately dispatch aid to the disaster site and assist in the search for survivors. Brazilian President Bolsonaro thanked the Prime Minister and accepted his offer.

The two agreed that an Israeli mission with rescue equipment would leave within 24 hours.
