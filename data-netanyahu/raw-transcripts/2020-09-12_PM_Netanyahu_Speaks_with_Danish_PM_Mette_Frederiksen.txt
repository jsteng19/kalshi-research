Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, spoke with Danish Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen and thanked her for her steadfast position in defense of the Jewish community and the ancient tradition of circumcision.Prime Minister Netanyahu noted that this was a matter of maintaining Jewish identity through the generations and that he appreciates how the Danish people protected the Jewish community during and after the Holocaust. He added that he hopes that this commitment will also continue in the future.The leaders also discussed dealing with the challenges of the coronavirus.
