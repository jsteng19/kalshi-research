Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, who is visiting the Netherlands, met earlier today with Dutch Deputy Prime Minister Maxime Verhagen and Foreign Minister Uri Rosenthal (a Jew married to an Israeli woman). The Prime Minister thanked them for the warm and brave ties between the two countries and for Dutch support of Israel in the international arena. After his meeting with Dutch Foreign Minister Rosenthal, the Prime Minister and Mrs. Netanyahu left to visit the Portuguese Synagogue in Amsterdam.
