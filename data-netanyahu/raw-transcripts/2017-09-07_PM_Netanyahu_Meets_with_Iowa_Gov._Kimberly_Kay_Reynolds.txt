Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today in Jerusalem, met with Iowa Governor Kimberly Kay Reynolds, who is leading a delegation to Israel. The two discussed ways to advance cooperation between Israel and Iowa especially in water, technology and agriculture. The Iowa delegation will sign two inter-university agreements, with Tel Aviv University and the Volcani Center.

Gov. Reynolds invited Prime Minister Netanyahu to visit Iowa.
