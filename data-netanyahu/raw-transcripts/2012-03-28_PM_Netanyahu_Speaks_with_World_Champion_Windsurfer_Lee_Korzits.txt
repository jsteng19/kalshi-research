Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this afternoon, spoke to windsurfer

Lee Korzits, who won the RS:X Windsurfing World Championship at the competition in Cadiz, Spain.

The Prime Minister spoke to her from the shores of the Sea of Galilee where, for the first time, the Ministerial Committee on Tiberias Affairs convened today, and said, "Congratulations Lee, you have honorably and successfully represented both yourself and the State of Israel. You are a champion, the best in the world. I and all of Israel hope that you will also win a gold medal at the London Olympics."
