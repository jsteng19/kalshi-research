Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Minister of Foreign Affairs Israel Katz have decided to appoint MK Danny Danon as Israeli Ambassador to the UN.

MK Danon has considerable experience in the international arena and there is no doubt that his talents and experience will find expression at this time.

The appointment will be submitted for Government approval after which Ambassador-designate Danon will begin his second term as Israeli Ambassador to the UN.'
