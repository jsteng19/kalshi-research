Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has decided to appoint Yuval Rotem as Acting Director General of the Foreign Ministry in place of Dore Gold.

Yuval Rotem has served in the Foreign Ministry for 30 years and currently serves as head of public diplomacy. He has previously served – inter alia – as Ambassador to Australia, Consul General in Los Angeles and head of the Foreign Minister's staff.
