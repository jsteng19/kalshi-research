Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu today, agreed with National Information Directorate head Dr. Yoaz Hendel on the termination of the latter's work. The Prime Minister thanked Dr. Hendel for his many contributions to the State of Israel both at home and abroad.

Liran Dan, who has served as head of the Prime Minister's Office Media and Spokesmen's Unit, will be appointed as head of the National Information Directorate.
