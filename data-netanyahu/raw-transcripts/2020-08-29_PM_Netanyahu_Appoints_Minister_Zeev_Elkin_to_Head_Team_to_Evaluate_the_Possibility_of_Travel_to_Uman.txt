In coordination with Health Minister Yuli Edelstein and national coronavirus project manager Prof. Ronni Gamzu, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this evening, has appointed Minister Zeev Elkin to head a team that will evaluate the possibility of an outline for travel to Uman, under the severe restrictions of the authorities in Ukraine. Health Minister Edelstein, Foreign Minister Gabi Ashkenazi, Interior Minister Aryeh Deri and Science Minister Yizhar Shai are the other members of the team.
