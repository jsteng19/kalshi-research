Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today , at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Hungarian Foreign Minister Peter Szijjarto.

Prime Minister Netanyahu at the start of the meeting:

"You are wonderful friends of Israel. You stood by us now against this cynical decision that was promoted in the EU in the Council of Ministers. We're very, very grateful that we have great friends and you are among them, right at the top of the list."
