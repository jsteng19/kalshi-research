Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with British Foreign Secretary Dominic Raab.

Prime Minister Netanyahu at the start of the meeting:

"It's good to see you again in better times and we'll work together to make them even better. Thank you and Prime Minister Johnson, Boris, for the staunch, unwavering support of our right to self-defense during the recent operation. It's much appreciated."

British Foreign Secretary Raab:

"I'm here as a friend as you know that's a great supporter of Israel and we're looking forward to talking about the bilateral relationship but also how we move things forward elsewhere. You can always count on us."

Israeli Ambassador to the UK Tzipi Hotovely also participated in the meeting.
