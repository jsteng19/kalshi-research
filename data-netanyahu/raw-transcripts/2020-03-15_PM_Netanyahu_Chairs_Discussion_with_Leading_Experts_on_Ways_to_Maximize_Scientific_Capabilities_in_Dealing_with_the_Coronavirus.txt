Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is now chairing a discussion with leading experts on additional ways to deal with the Coronavirus. Under consideration are ways to maximize scientific capabilities in order to advance the ability to deal with the Coronavirus. Among those participating in the discussion are the head of the National Security Council, the head of the Health Ministry Public Health Service, the director of the main Health Ministry virus laboratory, the head of the Kupat Cholim Clalit Research and Policy Planning Department, the head of the Biological Institute, the head of the Defense Ministry Administration for the Development of Weapons and Technological Infrastructure, the President of the Weizmann Institute and the chairman of the Economy and Industry Ministry Innovation Authority. The Cabinet meeting is expected to resume this evening.
