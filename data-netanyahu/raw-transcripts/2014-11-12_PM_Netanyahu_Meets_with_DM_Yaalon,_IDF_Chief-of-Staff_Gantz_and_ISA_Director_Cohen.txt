Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, this afternoon, in his Jerusalem office, met with Defense Minister Moshe Yaalon, IDF Chief-of-Staff Lt.-Gen. Benny Gantz and ISA Director Yoram Cohen and ordered an immediate halt to publicly dealing with issues that should be resolved between the security services. "We all have a national responsibility for the security of the State of Israel and we must continue to fully cooperate for the security of Israel's citizens," the Prime Minister said.
