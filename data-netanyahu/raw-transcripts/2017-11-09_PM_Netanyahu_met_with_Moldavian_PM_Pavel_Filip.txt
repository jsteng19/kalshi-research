PM Netanyahu met this morning with Moldavian PM Pavel Filip.

Prime Minister Netanyahu congratulated 25 years of relations between the two countries, and the willingness to continue deepening economic cooperation and trade expansion.

At the meeting the two discussed MASHAV's (Israel's agency for international development cooperation) aid in the fields of water, agriculture, green energy and medicine.
