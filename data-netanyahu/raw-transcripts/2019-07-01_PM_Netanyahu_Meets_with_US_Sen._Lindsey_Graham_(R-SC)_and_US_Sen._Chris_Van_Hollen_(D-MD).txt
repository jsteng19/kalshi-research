Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with US Sen. Lindsey Graham (R-SC) and US Sen. Chris Van Hollen (D-MD). They discussed regional issues, including Iran and Syria. The Prime Minister noted the importance of increasing the pressure on Iran. The senators stressed the continuation of bipartisan support for Israel.
