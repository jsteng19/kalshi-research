Ahead of the meetings of the Housing Cabinet and the Committee on Earthquake Preparedness next week, Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today, held two discussions: A comprehensive discussion on lowering housing prices, including preferences for reservist soldiers, and a discussion on providing solutions for earthquakes in high-risk communities. These two issues will be discussed at the meetings next week. Also participating in the discussions were Construction and Housing Minister Yitzhak Goldknopf, Construction and Housing Ministry Director General Yehuda Morgenstern, Israel Land Authority Director General Yaakov Quint, Prime Minister's Office Director General Yossi Shelley, National Economic Council Chairman Prof. Avi Simhon and Construction and Housing Ministry Chief of Staff Motti Babchik.
