PM Netanyahu will leave for Brussels shortly where he will meet with US Secretary of State Mike Pompeo this evening; the meeting was set last week. The two will discuss regional developments.

Prime Minister Netanyahu will be accompanied by Mossad Director Yossi Cohen, National Security Council Director Meir Ben-Shabbat, and his Military Secretary, Brig.-Gen. Avi Blot.
