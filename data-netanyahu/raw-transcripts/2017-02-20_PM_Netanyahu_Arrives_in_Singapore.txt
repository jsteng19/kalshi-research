Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara were welcomed by Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong and his wife at the Istana (official residence of the President of Singapore). Prime Minister Netanyahu inspected an honor guard and signed the official guestbook; the national anthems were also played.

Prime Minister Netanyahu then met with Singapore President Dr. Tony Tan Keng Yam; ministers from both countries also attended the meeting. Among the topics discussed were deepening bilateral cooperation in technology in order to overcome common challenges as well assistance to African countries in the fields of technology and initiatives.

The Singapore President noted that both nations started as small countries without natural resources and developed technologies that aided national growth and prosperity. Prime Minister Netanyahu raised the issue of opening a direct air route between the two countries and invited his host to visit Israel.

Prime Minister Netanyahu is due to hold a private meeting with Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong after which the leaders will issue statements.
