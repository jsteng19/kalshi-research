Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu spoke with Esther Pollard following the announcement of Jonathan Pollard's parole.

Prime Minister Netanyahu: "After decades of effort, Jonathan Pollard will finally be released. Throughout his time in prison, I consistently raised the issue of his release in my meetings and conversations with the leadership of successive U.S. administrations. We are looking forward to his release."
