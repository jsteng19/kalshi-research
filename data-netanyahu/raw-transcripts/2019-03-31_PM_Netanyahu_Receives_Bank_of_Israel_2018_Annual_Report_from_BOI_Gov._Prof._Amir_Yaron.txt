Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, at the Prime Minister’s Office in Jerusalem, received the Bank of Israel 2018 Annual Report from Bank of Israel Governor Prof. Amir Yaron. BOI Gov. Prof. Yaron: “I am pleased to present you with the Bank of Israel 2018 Annual Report. This was a good year for the Israeli economy, which is in a good Shape. The report reviews many aspects of the Israeli economy, monetary policy, social benefits and the balance of payments, as well as short-range fiscal challenges and opportunities to improve long-term growth.” PM Netanyahu: “I am pleased to hear it. The economy is in a good Shape because our policy is good. We reached a unique achievement in which unemployment is at an all-time low and wages are rising rapidly. This combination, as you know, is unusual and not self-evident, but it is a great achievement.”
