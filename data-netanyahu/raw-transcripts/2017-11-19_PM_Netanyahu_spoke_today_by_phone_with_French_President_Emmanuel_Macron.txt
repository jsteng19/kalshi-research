PM Netanyahu spoke this afternoon by phone with French President Emmanuel Macron.

The conversation lasted about half an hour and was scheduled at request of the French president.

President Macron briefed Prime Minister Netanyahu on the measures he is taking in the crisis in Lebanon.

The leaders decided to speak again in the coming days and meet in Paris at the beginning of next month.

Prime Minister Netanyahu and President Macron discussed the nuclear deal with Iran, Iran's attempts to establish itself in Syria and its activities in the region.
