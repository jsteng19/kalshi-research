Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu this morning expressed his deep sorrow over the passing of Center of Organizations of Holocaust Survivors in Israel Chairman Noah Flug. The Prime Minister said that, "Throughout his life Flug contributed greatly to Holocaust survivors in Israel. A representative of concentration camp survivors, Flug dedicated his life to serving the public and worked towards the welfare of Holocaust survivors in Israel."
