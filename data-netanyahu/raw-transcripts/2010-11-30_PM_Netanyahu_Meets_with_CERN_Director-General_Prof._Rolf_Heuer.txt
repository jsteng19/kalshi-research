Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu met this morning (Tuesday), 30.11.10, with

European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN)

Director-General Rolf Heuer, who is currently visiting Israel as a guest of the

Weizmann Institute of Science

CERN Dir.-Gen. Heuer briefed Prime Minister Netanyahu on his organization and expressed his appreciation for Israel's scientific achievements.

Prime Minister Netanyahu said that he welcomes the fruitful cooperation between Israel and CERN, which is art the forefront of global scientific research. He noted that Israel has been working with the organization for many years, that many Israeli scientists have worked there and that Israeli companies compete in its tenders.
