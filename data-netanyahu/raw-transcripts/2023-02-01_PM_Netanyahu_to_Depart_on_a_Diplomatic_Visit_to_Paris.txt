Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu will tomorrow afternoon, depart on a diplomatic visit to Paris where he will meet with French President Emmanuel Macron at the Elysee Palace; this is in continuation of their conversation this past Sunday evening. The two leaders will discuss diplomatic issues on the agenda, especially the international effort to stop the Iranian nuclear program and ways to strengthen and expand the Abraham Accords. The two leaders have good and cordial relations dating from Prime Minister Netanyahu's previous term. They last met in Jerusalem in January 2020. Prime Minister Netanyahu will also meet with leading French businesspeople in the area of finance and with Jewish community leaders.
