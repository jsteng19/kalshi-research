Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu sends his Condolences to the Kennedy family and the American people, and joins in their sorrow on the passing of Senator Ted Kennedy.

Upon being informed of Senator Kennedy's passing, Prime Minister Netanyahu said:

"Ted had been a friend for the last 30 years. He was one of the greatest patriots to arise from the American nation, a special person and a profound friend to Israel and to the Jewish People. He will be sorely missed."
