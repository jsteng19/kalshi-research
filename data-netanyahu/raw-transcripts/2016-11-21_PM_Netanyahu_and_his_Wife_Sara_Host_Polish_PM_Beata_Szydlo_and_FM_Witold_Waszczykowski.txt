Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara this evening at the Prime Minister's Residence in Jerusalem, hosted dinner for Polish Prime Minister Beata Szydlo and Polish Foreign Minister Witold Waszczykowski.

Prime Minister Netanyahu told his Polish counterpart that he was happy to meet her again and welcomed her to Jerusalem.

Polish Prime Minister Szydlo signed the guestbook. She thanked Prime Minister Netanyahu for the meeting and said that it was a great honor to be hosted at his home. She noted that Poland and Israel were two countries with a shared past and a shared future.

Tomorrow the Israeli and Polish governments will hold their third G2G meeting, the goal of which is to deepen bilateral relations, sign agreements and strengthen cooperation in economic matters, science and technology, cyber, defense, security and culture.
