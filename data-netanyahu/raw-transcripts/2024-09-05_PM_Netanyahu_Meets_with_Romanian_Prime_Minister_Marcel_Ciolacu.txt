Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, Foreign Affairs Minister Israel Katz, Economy Minister Nir Barkat and a representative from the National Security Council, today, at the Prime Minister's Office in Jerusalem, met with Romanian Prime Minister Marcel Ciolacu, Foreign Affairs Minister Luminița Odobescu, National Defence Minister Angel Tilvar and Economy, Entrepreneurship and Tourism Minister Ștefan-Radu Oprea.

Prime Minister Netanyahu thanked Romanian Prime Minister Ciolacu for his willingness to help in the efforts to free the hostages, including those with Romanian citizenship, and for his offer to assist in the humanitarian effort.
