Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, today (Monday, 20 July 2020), held a discussion on an outline for activity of the education system with the Defense, Education and Science and Technology ministers, the head of the National Security Council, the Director General of the Health Ministry, the Acting Director General of the Prime Minister’s Office, the head of the National Economic Council, the Chairman of the Federation of Local Authorities, the Chairman of the Merhavim Regional Council and other officials. It was decided that ‘summer vacation schools’ will not be closed at this stage. An additional assessment will be held on Thursday, 23 July, at which all possibilities on the agenda will be discussed.
