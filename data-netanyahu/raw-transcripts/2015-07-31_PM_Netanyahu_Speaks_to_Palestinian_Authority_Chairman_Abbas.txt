Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, a short while ago today, spoke with Palestinian Authority Chairman Mahmoud Abbas and said that everyone in Israel was shocked by the reprehensible terrorism against the Dawabsha family. The Prime Minister added: "We must fight terrorism together regardless of which side it comes from," and noted that he had ordered the security forces to use all measures to locate the murderers.
