Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and his wife Sara this evening were welcomed by UK Chief Rabbi Ephraim Mirvis and UK Jewish community leaders, who briefed them on the difficulties and challenges they face as defenders of Israel.

Among those present wasUnion of Jewish Students President Hannah Brady who described how students, in cooperation with other organizations, were fighting the BDS movement in an effort to block it from spreading. She noted that the BDS movement exists on only approximately 5% of British campuses.

Prime Minister Netanyahu urged those present to fight back and not apologize. He said that this is an important struggle for public opinion that must be waged with determination and by pointing out that Israel – the only democracy in the Middle East – is at the forefront of the struggle against extremist Islam.
