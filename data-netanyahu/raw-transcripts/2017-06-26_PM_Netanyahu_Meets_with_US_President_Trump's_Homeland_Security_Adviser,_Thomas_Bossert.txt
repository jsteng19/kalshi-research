Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu met with US President Donald Trump's Homeland Security Adviser, Thomas P. Bossert, and with White House Cybersecurity Advisor Rob Joyce.

It was agreed to establish joint bilateral working groups on – inter alia – international cyber policy, critical infrastructure defense, research and development, and human capital development.

The establishment of the working groups is in the wake of the meeting between Prime Minister Netanyahu and US President Trump.
