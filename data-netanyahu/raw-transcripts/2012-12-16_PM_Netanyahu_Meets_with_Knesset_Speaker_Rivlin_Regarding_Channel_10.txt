Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Knesset Speaker Reuven Rivlin, this evening (Sunday, 16 December 2012), agreed that the Knesset would convene in the coming days to discuss the proposed agreement regarding the continued operation of Channel 10, which was formulated by a professional team that included Prime Minister's Office Director-General Harel Locker, Finance Ministry Accountant-General Michal Abadi-Boiangiu, and Finance Ministry Budget Director Gal Hershkovitz.

PMO Dir.-Gen. Locker and the Channel 2 licensees' director-general will also discuss the licensees' request for easements in the wake of the crisis in the communications market.

The Knesset will also discuss a six-month extension of local news broadcasts and approving an extension of the tenure of Deputy Foreign Minister Danny Ayalon.
