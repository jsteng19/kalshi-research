PM Netanyahu, in Davos, met with Rwandan President Paul Kagame.

The Prime Minister congratulated President Kagame as he assumes the Chairmanship of the African Union. The two leaders discussed a range of issues, including expanding cooperation between Israel and Rwanda.

Regarding the migrant issue, PM Netanyahu agreed with President Kagame, who made clear that he would only accept a process that fully complies with international law.
